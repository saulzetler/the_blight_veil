## **Session 1 Summary**

**Date:** October 28, 2025
**Location:** Orrenval, the capital of the Concord
**Attendees:** Dawson Markle, Kyle Sellars, Saul Zetler, Paul Bridi, Lo McEwan

---

### **Opening Scene – The Town Square**

* The session begins in **Orrenval’s central square**, during a public heresy trial.
* **Cassie Thongington** prompts introductions among the party to establish familiarity.

#### **Introductions**

* **Ashton Zennor** – Gruff ex-Concord soldier from Orrenval’s rougher districts.
* **Fergalous (Fergie)** – Devout follower of the *Temple of Radiant Dawn*.
* **Unnamed Robot** – Newly awakened construct, unaware of how long they’ve been dormant.
* **Hog** – Scrappy, tight-lipped dwarf.

---

### **The Trial of Marla**

* **Marla** is accused of **heresy** for singing a forbidden melody.
* She is brought forth by **Captain Deyran** and **Inquisitor Rellis**, destined for the pyre.
* The party quickly shares their disdain for the Concord and decides to intervene.
* Deyran demands immediate punishment; Rellis suggests hearing the party out.
* Deyran insists on *justice by sword and flame*.
* **Hog** proposes *trial by combat*, resulting in a **4v4 battle**.

---

### **Combat & Escape**

* During combat:

  * Marla is nearly killed by an *Ice Knife* explosion and flees.
  * Rellis calls for reinforcements; soldiers block off the exits.
  * A **shadowy figure** appears and beckons the party to follow.
  * **Captain Deyran** is slain by the Unnamed Robot’s **Moonbeam**.
* The group escapes under concealment magic into the alleyways.

---

### **The Cindersong Hideout**

* The shadowy figure leads them to a **hidden base**, marked by a sigil:
  **three rings bound together within flame**.
* He introduces himself as **Vaereth**, a member of **Cindersong**.
* Vaereth warns that killing a Concord captain will bring intense pursuit.
* He offers **three escape routes**:

  1. **East – The Ashroot:** forests, rebels, druids, and villagers under Concord threat.
  2. **South – The Godscar:** desolate, war-torn wasteland.
  3. **North – The Coast:** rumored merchant town, little known.
* The party chooses to head **east toward the Ashroot**.
* **Marla** is taken away to recover.
* They rest in the hideout, told to leave before dawn.

#### **Character Aspirations**

* **Fergie:** Seeks a master of fire.
* **Unnamed Robot:** Wants to find their creator and learn why they were deactivated.

---

### **Escape from Orrenval**

* At dawn, the party discovers **rumors** branding them as clumsy, worthless criminals.
* The hideout is deserted.
* Around **4 AM**, they sneak through the empty streets to the **eastern gate**.
* Two guards block the way.

#### **Distraction Plan**

* The Unnamed Robot summons a **raven familiar** — considered an *ill omen* in Orrenval.
* One guard leaves to destroy the raven; Zennor shoots the other from a rooftop.
* The guards are swiftly defeated, and the party escapes through the gate.

---

### **The Veil Event**

* As they flee, they witness a **supernatural phenomenon**:

  * A **thin vertical tear** opens in the air, revealing a distorted, overexposed realm.
  * A **veiled silhouette** presses its hand against the barrier.
  * A whisper echoes in their minds:

    > “The veil... remembers what it was made to forget.”
  * The tear seals shut, releasing a **pulse of force** that extinguishes every torch in sight.
  * **The Sanctara’s bells** begin to toll.

---

### **Session Close**

* The group inspects the Unnamed Robot and finds a serial number: **05C4R**.
* They nickname him **Oscar**.
