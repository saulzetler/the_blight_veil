# **The Faith**

**Overview:**  
The Faith is the spiritual spine of the known world — a monolithic institution fractured by doubt. Its followers worship the *Silent Pantheon*, gods who turned mute after the Blight’s arrival. Once a force of unity, the Faith now teeters between redemption and fanaticism, each sect interpreting divine silence as either punishment or liberation.

---

## **Doctrine and Belief**

The Faith teaches that the Veil is the gods’ test — a divine crucible to burn away weakness. Its clergy preach that only through obedience, ritual, and sacrifice can the gods be stirred to speech once more. Yet cracks run deep: not all agree on what the silence means.

- **The Dawn Orthodoxy:** Believes the gods’ silence is a test of faith, demanding purity and obedience.
- **The Ashen Veil:** Teaches that humanity’s sins deafened the gods, and only through penance can the world be redeemed.
- **The Veilborn Heresy:** Claims the Blighted are divine messengers, not curses.

---

## **Leadership**

### **Archon Seraphine Vale** — *The Dawn Voice*  
A charismatic preacher and master orator, Seraphine leads the Faith’s largest orthodox branch. Her honeyed words conceal ruthless conviction. She preaches that faith is light — and all who cast shadow must be purged.

**Principles:** Purity through devotion; faith as the shield of civilization.  
**Allegiances:** Supports the Concord publicly, but sees herself as the true moral authority.  
**Notable Events:** Led the restoration of the Sunspire Basilica; personally sanctioned the execution of the Heretic Prophet.

---

### **Mother Rhenna of the Ashen Veil**  
A gaunt, ascetic mystic who claims to have heard echoes of the gods within the Blight’s storms. Her followers scourge themselves in ritual repentance, wandering the wastelands to suffer on behalf of mankind.

**Principles:** Redemption through pain and silence.  
**Allegiances:** Secretly trades knowledge with the Blighted, believing their transformation is divine punishment.  
**Notable Events:** Founded the Order of the Ashen Path; witnessed the Burning of the Oracle Towers.

---

## **The Inquisition of the Veil**

The Faith’s militant arm — inquisitors trained to identify heresy, mutation, and forbidden magic. They are both feared and respected, operating independently but sanctioned by the Concord when convenient.

---

## **Relations with Other Factions**

- **The Concord:** An uneasy partnership; the Faith provides moral legitimacy, the Concord offers protection.  
- **The Blighted:** Declared abominations, though some sects secretly seek to understand them.  
- **The Free Peoples:** Viewed as heathens — yet Faith missionaries often seek to convert them at the frontier.
