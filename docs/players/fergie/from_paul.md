Name: Fegulous (or Fergie).  
Race: Fire Genasi 
Class: Life Domain 
Alignment: Neutral Neutral I was born to a long line of blacksmiths. 

I was born to a long line of blacksmiths. I, unlike my family though, was born with supernatural fire powers. My father was an honest hard-working man, but felt it would be better for my future that I be raised outside of the Blacksmith ‘circuit’ - he left me to be brought up and educated in a monastery outside of town - the Temple of Radiant Dawn - from childhood. 

This sanctuary was devoted to the healing light of the Morninglord, Lathander. The temple’s priests taught me that life is the most sacred flame, and that fire was not only about destruction, but about life and rebirth. Over time, my relationship with fire deepened - my fire powers manifested themselves in line with how I was feeling. The priests encouraged his devotion, but whispered of his strange gift: whenever my emotions surged, so too did fire flicker at my fingertips. Anger singed wood. Joy lit candles without touch. Sadness left trails of smoke. I embraced these powers, and made a promise to myself that when my life would take me beyond my temple’s walls, I would learn to develop them and learn more about their nature. 

My special item is my “porter-stone”: a stone that town’s people choose at the age of 16 (really, it chooses you) in a coming of age ritual. Mine is a black opal, which in my town’s lore is said to have magical properties. Like many others in my culture, all my jewelry has attachments where I can place the stone - usually to my ring, but also my earrings, necklaces, anklet...

After years of service in my Temple - doing tasks like changing bandages, carrying water, comforting the sick. And learning the foundations of worshipping Life and servitude as a cleric, I’ve decided to leave my Temple for the Concord, where a wayward traveller that I once helped told me of an old Fire Genasi scholar that I could perhaps learn from or ask questions.
