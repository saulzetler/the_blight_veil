# 🎭 Scene: “When the Veil Breathes”

### ⚙️ Setup (Transition)

> The crowd begins to scatter from the square, murmuring prayers and superstitions as the Concord’s clerics usher them away.
> The last orange light of dusk slides across the marble facades of Orrenval’s heart — white and gold turned to copper and blood.
>
> You stand amid the echo of the day’s spectacle. The banners still flutter, torn by the wind. The air smells of burnt incense and iron.

If the players were involved in any conflict, interrogation, or side task here, this is the moment it settles — the quiet before something breaks.

---

### 🌑 The Stillness

> A sudden stillness falls.
>
> The chatter, the wind, even the faint ringing of the Sanctara’s bells — all stop.
>
> The silence isn’t peaceful. It’s *vacant.* Like the world has exhaled and forgotten to breathe in again.

🧩 **Perception Check (DC 13):**
A successful character feels a low vibration in their feet — a thrumming pulse from the stone beneath them, like a heartbeat echoing through the marble.

🧩 **Arcana or Religion Check (DC 15):**
A faint, unnatural static prickles across the skin. Those attuned to divine or arcane forces sense something *pressing against reality itself*, thin and hungry.

---

### ⚡ The Distortion

> The light changes — not fading, but *bending.*
>
> Shadows twist and lengthen toward the center of the square, where moments ago the ritual took place. They converge, like black ink drawn toward a drain.
>
> Then, the air *cracks.*

Describe the fissure visually here — slow, deliberate, unsettling:

> A thin, vertical tear splits open in midair — no wider than a handspan, but impossibly deep. Inside, the world looks… wrong.
> Not dark, but *overexposed*, like staring into a reflection of stars drowning beneath an ocean.
>
> The sound that follows is not thunder — it’s a **reversed breath.** The air is being *inhaled*.

🧩 **Constitution Save (DC 12):**
Those who fail stumble or choke as the air pressure drops. Those who pass feel their lungs ache, as if the world itself is holding its breath through them.

---

### 🩸 The Manifestation

> The fissure widens. Pale light leaks through the tear, shimmering like oil on water.
>
> Then, something moves within it — a *silhouette*.
> A tall, veiled figure stands just beyond the tear, visible for an instant through the light. Its face cannot be seen. Its hand presses against the barrier — almost tenderly.
>
> A whisper fills your mind, layered and distant:
>
> > “The veil... remembers what it was made to forget.”

🧩 **Wisdom Save (DC 14):**
Fail: The PC glimpses flashes of alien memories — mountains collapsing into mist, oceans of black glass, a crowd kneeling before an eclipse. They take 1 point of psychic damage and feel watched.
Success: They resist, but hear the voice echoing faintly for several seconds longer.

---

### 🌌 The Collapse

> The fissure *snaps shut.*
> A pulse of force rolls outward — dust lifts from the marble, banners whip in all directions, and every torch within sight gutters out at once.
>
> Then — silence again.

🧩 **Investigation or Arcana Check (DC 15):**
The stone at the center of the square is **blackened**, veins of obsidian threading out like cracks. The air smells faintly of ozone and incense.
Touching it makes the skin *numb.*

---

### 🔔 The Aftermath

> The Sanctara’s bells begin to toll.
>
> No one pulled their ropes.
> The sound is uneven, discordant — one bell lags behind the others, creating an off-beat rhythm like a staggered heartbeat.
>
> From the chapel steps, a lone Concord priest drops to their knees, eyes wide with terror.
>
> > “The Veil… *breathes again.*”

If you want to leave them with a final sting:

> The priest looks up at you — recognition, or accusation, in their eyes.
> They whisper:
>
> > “And it saw *you.*”

---

### 🪶 Optional Hooks

* **Concord Suppression:** Moments later, Concord enforcers arrive. They seal the square, confiscate notes, and order silence. “No word of this leaves Orrenval.”
* **Arcane Residue:** The amulet (or any magical trinket) glows faintly, as if *resonating* with what just happened.
* **Missing Witnesses:** By morning, those who stayed in the square that night have vanished, their names already omitted from records.

---

### 🎬 End of Session

> Fade to black.
>
> The sound of the Sanctara’s bells continues long after the screen goes dark — until, one by one, they fall silent again.
