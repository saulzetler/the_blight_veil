# Session 2 Ideas

Based on the possible outcomes and choices from Session 1, here are prompts and ideas for Session 2. Each path leads to a unique scenario, allowing you to build on the consequences of the players' actions.

## If the Players Intervened Publicly (Confrontation)
- **Prompt:** The party has fled with Marla into the alleys, pursued by Concord patrols. The city is on high alert, and wanted posters bearing their likenesses appear overnight. The Cindersong offers sanctuary, but demands a favor in return: sabotage a Concord ritual or rescue another sympathizer from captivity.
- **Session 2 Ideas:**
  - Urban chase and stealth scenes as the party evades capture.
  - Planning and executing a sabotage or rescue mission for the Cindersong.
  - Navigating a city under lockdown, dealing with informants, and making alliances with other rebels or outcasts.

## If the Players Negotiated for Marla’s Release
- **Prompt:** The party is marked as suspect but not openly hunted. The Concord watches their every move, and the Faith questions their motives. Marla, now in their custody, reveals secrets about the Cindersong and the true nature of the hymn.
- **Session 2 Ideas:**
  - Investigating the origins and power of the Cindersong hymn.
  - Dealing with Concord surveillance and subtle threats from the Faith.
  - Deciding whether to help Marla spread her message or keep her hidden.

## If the Players Rescued Marla via Stealth/Deception
- **Prompt:** The party reaches a safehouse, but Concord agents and rival factions are searching for them. The Cindersong recognizes their skill and asks them to infiltrate a Concord stronghold to retrieve a vital artifact or document.
- **Session 2 Ideas:**
  - Planning and executing a heist or infiltration mission.
  - Interacting with Cindersong contacts and learning more about their network.
  - Facing moral dilemmas about what to do with the information or artifact they recover.

## If the Players Did Nothing / Observed
- **Prompt:** The party is marked as witnesses and potential recruits by the Cindersong, but also as suspicious by the Concord. They receive a coded message inviting them to the catacombs, where a secret meeting is held.
- **Session 2 Ideas:**
  - Attending a clandestine gathering in the catacombs, meeting key Cindersong figures.
  - Being offered a choice: join the resistance, inform on them, or walk away.
  - Uncovering new rumors and secrets about the Blight Veil and the city’s underbelly.

## General Session 2 Hooks
- The city remains tense, with rumors of rebellion and increased Concord patrols.
- NPCs from Session 1 (Marla, Rellis, Deyran, Cindersong sympathizers) reappear with new motives and information.
- The party’s reputation and choices affect how they are treated by various factions.
- New opportunities arise for alliances, betrayals, and uncovering deeper mysteries about the Blight Veil and the Concord’s secrets.

---
Use these prompts to tailor Session 2 to your players’ choices, ensuring that their actions have meaningful consequences and open up new storylines.
