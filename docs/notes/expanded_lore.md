# Expanded Lore & Mysteries

## Mythologies & Legends
- **The Godscar:** A forbidden region said to be the resting place of slain gods. No one returns unchanged.
- **The Song of the Lost:** A tale of a bard who wandered the Veil and returned with a melody that can heal or destroy.

## Magical Phenomena
- **Veil Anomalies:** Places where time loops, dreams become real, or the dead speak. Some are mapped, most are feared.
- **Legendary Artifacts:**
  - *The Lantern of Dawn:* Said to reveal truth and banish illusions.
  - *Ashroot Seed:* A relic that can restore corrupted land.

## Unresolved Mysteries
- What secrets do the Veilward Obelisks truly hold?
- Who was the First Veilborn, and what became of them?
- Why do some regions resist the Veil while others succumb?
- What happened to the first Concord expedition into the Godscar?
- Are the Cindersong’s melodies truly magical, or just dangerous ideas?

## Timeline of Major Events
- **The Godfall:** The war that shattered the divine and birthed the Veil.
- **Founding of the Concord:** Unification under the Trifarix.
- **Ashroot Rebellion:** The last stand of the forest shamans.
- **The Blighted Exodus:** Survivors fleeing the Veil’s advance.
- **The Merchant’s Pact:** Free Cities unite to resist Concord control.
