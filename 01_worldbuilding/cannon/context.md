# World Structure

## The Concord

- Central ruling body of the known political world.
    
- Authoritarian, structured, ideologically rigid.
    

### The Trifarix

The ruling body of the Concord.

Three leaders, each representing one pillar:

1. **Might**
    
2. **Vision**
    
3. **Guile**
    

This structure is absolute canon.

---

## Orrenval

- Capital city of the Concord.
    
- Political and ideological center of power.
    

### The Sanctara

- The holy chapel of the Concord.
    
- Religious and symbolic heart of Orrenval.

# Campaign History (Established Events)

## The Inciting Crime

- The party **killed Captain Deyran** in Orrenval.
    
- They fled the capital immediately after.
    

This event is fixed and cannot be retconned without deliberate decision.

---

## The Ashroot

- A forest region made up of scattered villages.
    
- Outside direct Concord control (degree of control may evolve).
    

### Rootspire

- Major city within Ashroot.
    
- Also known as **The Great Tree City**.
    

---

## The Council of 8

- The party faced trial in Rootspire.
    
- The Council consists of **eight beings**.
    
- Nature of the Council:
    
    - Potentially godly
        
    - Potentially beyond the Veil
        
    - Definitively mysterious
        

No further truths are assumed unless explicitly added here.
