# Factions Index

## [The Blighted](factions/blighted_faction.md)
Corrupted by the Veil, the Blighted are outcasts transformed by its energy. Shunned and feared, they survive through adaptation and possess strange abilities. Led by the mythic First Veilborn, they seek meaning in their cursed existence.

## [The Concord](factions/concord_faction.md)
The Concord is the dominant regime, ruled by the Trifarix Council of Three: Might, Vision, and Guile. They maintain order and civilization against the Blight, valuing discipline, strength, and intellect above all.

## [The Faith](factions/faith_faction.md)
The Faith is a fractured spiritual institution worshipping the Silent Pantheon. Its sects debate the meaning of divine silence after the Blight, ranging from strict orthodoxy to heretical acceptance of the Blighted.

## [The Free Peoples](factions/free_peoples_faction.md)
A confederation of exiles, nomads, and survivors who reject the Concord and Faith. They value freedom, cunning, and loyalty, living by their own rules and preserving lost traditions in a world scarred by the Veil.
