## 🌍 Hometown & Placement in the World

* **Hometown:** **Emberhold**, a mining and smithing settlement built along the slopes of the volcanic ridge *Ashenpeak* in western **Cindralith**. Emberhold is known for its master smiths who work with rare ores tempered in volcanic forges. Many famous weapons and Concord relics have passed through its fires.
* **Temple of Radiant Dawn:** Located just outside Emberhold, the monastery perches atop the cliffs, overlooking molten flows below. This temple is both sanctuary and forge-shrine — its priests believe that every spark is a reflection of Lathander’s dawn.

## 🔥 Major Events Tied to Backstory

1. **The Scorching Procession**
   When Fegulous was 10, the town of Emberhold experienced a strange omen: during the annual forging festival, the sky turned crimson, and fire genasi children (rare as they are) all displayed sparks of uncontrolled flame. Some whispered this was tied to the Blight Veil’s slow corruption, others that it was Lathander’s blessing. Fergie’s emotional flares during this event made them both feared and revered locally.
   *Tie-in:* The Concord keeps records of these omens — meaning Fegulous’s powers may already be known to them.

2. **The Veil-Touched Pilgrim**
   Years later, while at the Temple, Fegulous tended to a wounded traveler who had wandered from the edges of the Blight Veil. This traveler, half-broken but full of strange tales, whispered of a *“song of fire buried beneath the ashlands”*. They mentioned a fire genasi scholar in the Concord capital, one who “hears the same fire you do.”
   *Tie-in:* This is what set Fergie on the path to the capital — and the PCs may later discover the traveler was a Cindersong sympathizer.

## 💎 The Porter-Stone

* **Name:** *The Ember’s Heart* (Black Opal).
* **Lore:** Every youth in Emberhold undergoes the *Choosing of the Stone*. It is believed the stones are fragments of a primordial forge where the gods once shaped the world. Fergie’s black opal is particularly rare; miners whisper it comes from beneath Ashenpeak, where flame and shadow mingle.
* **Symbolic Significance:** In Emberhold, black opals are tied to prophecy — it is said a black opal-bearer will either *ignite a new dawn* or *burn the world to ash*.
* **Possible Magical Property (low-level, grows with story):**

  * *Dormant:* While carried, the stone glows faintly when near Blight Veil corruption (like a mini Geiger counter).
  * *Awakened (later):* Once per long rest, as a reaction, Fergie can reroll a failed saving throw against fire or radiant damage.
  * *Fully Awakened (major arc):* Grants resistance to necrotic damage when within the Blight Veil.

## 🎭 Campaign Integration

* **Session 1 Hook:**
  Marla (the heretic on trial) hums a fragment of the *Cindersong* that causes Fergie’s opal to pulse with warmth. It is the first time the stone has reacted to anything outside Emberhold.
* **Cindersong Connection:**
  The Cindersong believes the black opal is a “key-stone” to unlocking their Hymn of Fire — an artifact or ritual weapon against the Concord. This will put Fergie squarely in the middle of both factions.
* **Concord Connection:**
  Inquisitor Rellis has seen Fergie’s name in Concord records from Emberhold’s omens — he may recognize the stone, and suspect that Fergie is far more than “just another cleric.”
