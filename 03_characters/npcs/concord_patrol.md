## Captain Verrin Kael – Concord Commander

**Medium humanoid (human), lawful neutral**  
**CR 4 (1,100 XP)**

---

**Armor Class:** 17 (plate, radiant bulwark field)  
**Hit Points:** 70 (8d8 + 32)  
**Speed:** 30 ft

---

**STR** 17 (+3) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 18 (+4) **INT** 11 (+0) **WIS** 13 (+1) **CHA** 15 (+2)

---

**Saving Throws** Con +6, Wis +3  
**Skills** Athletics +5, Intimidation +4, Perception +3  
**Senses** passive Perception 13  
**Languages** Common, Concord Cant  
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)

---

### Traits

**Tactical Commander.** As a bonus action, Kael can order one ally within 30 ft to move up to half its speed or make a weapon attack.

**Radiant Bulwark.** The first 5 radiant damage Kael would take each round is reduced to 0.

**Lantern Line.** Three hovering lanterns (AC 13 HP 10 each) emit 20 ft bright/20 ft dim light. At the start of his turn, Kael can move one lantern up to 15 ft. Allies in bright light gain +1 AC; enemies starting their turn there take 2 radiant damage (DC 12 Con save for half).

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Two longsword attacks.  
**Longsword.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. _Hit:_ 8 (1d8 + 3) slashing + 3 (1d6) radiant.

**Radiant Pulse (Recharge 5–6).** Each lantern flashes. All creatures within 10 ft of a lantern make a DC 13 Con save or take 10 (3d6) radiant damage and are **blinded** until the start of Kael’s next turn.

### Legendary-Style Actions (2 per round)

1. **Commanding Radiance.** Move one lantern 15 ft and trigger a DC 13 Con save for 1d8 radiant damage (no blind).
    
2. **Parry & Riposte.** +2 AC until next turn; if a melee attack misses him, he makes one attack back.
    
3. **Hold the Line!** One ally within 30 ft may make a weapon attack or move half speed without provoking.
    

### Phase 2 — Blight Infusion (trigger < 30 HP)

Kael shatters a vial of blight.

- Gains +2 AC (19 total), +10 temporary HP.
    
- Melee deals +1d6 necrotic damage.
    
- Takes 5 damage per round (start of turn) as corruption eats him.
    
- _Lantern Line_ shifts to violet: zones deal **necrotic** instead of radiant, and _Radiant Pulse_ becomes _Veil Surge_—inverts light to darkness for 1 round (creatures in darkness are **frightened** DC 13 Wis save end of turn).
