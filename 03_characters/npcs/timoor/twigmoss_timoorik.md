
# 🌱 NPC: **Twigmoss Timoorik (“Timoor” for short)**

A tiny Root-Hollow Feyling who smells faintly of warm bark and sap-sugar.  
(Pronounced: Tih-MOOR-ick)

## Quick Profile

- **Race:** Root-Hollow Feyling (custom small fey)
    
- **Appearance:** 2½ feet tall, round moss-green fur with soft golden freckles that glow faintly when he’s excited. Big leaf-frond ears. A little root-woven satchel with carved wooden charms.
    
- **Vibe:** Teemo + Jester (EdExp33) + a well-meaning apprentice druid.
    
- **Personality:** Cheerful, brave in the wrong ways, deeply loyal, tries too hard, easily flustered, climbs people like he's a squirrel.
    
- **Role:** A helper who WANTS to be brave, but is actually more of a guide, medic, and lore-hint character.
    

---

# 🌿 Tiny Backstory (Player-Facing)

Timoor grew up in the **Root-Hollows**, the web of natural tunnels that braid around and through the central tree of Rootspire. Feylings like him rarely leave their hollow, but Timoor has a dream:  
**He wants to join the “High Branch Wardens,” the elite protectors of Rootspire.**  
But he’s… very small. And very distractible. And his crossbow fires seeds that _sometimes_ sprout instead of hitting anything.

He’s out in the open world to “collect deeds of helpfulness,” which he believes will earn him a place in the Wardens.

No one has told him this is not a real requirement.

---

# 🌑 Secret Lore (DM Only)

Timoor unconsciously carries an **unhatched Veil-Seed**, a micro-anomaly born from the torn Veil. It hums softly inside him like a second heartbeat.  
He doesn’t know this.

The Veil-Seed:

- subtly influences his luck
    
- draws him toward larger Veil-Core anomalies
    
- could bloom into a powerful boon _or_ a curse depending on how the party treats him
    

Later Revelation Options:

1. **He was “chosen” by the Rootspire itself** as a vessel for the seed.
    
2. **The Concord would absolutely imprison him** if they discovered it.
    
3. The seed reacts uniquely to the party’s harmonics, meaning **he is meant to travel with them**—or at least cross paths multiple times.

# 🏙️ First Introduction Scene (Inside Rootspire)

## **Read-Aloud**

> A sudden rustle from a woven-root market stall draws your attention.  
> A tiny moss-green creature pops up, wide-eyed, clutching an armful of glowing sap-orbs.
> 
> “Oh! Uh—hello! You’re tall. And armed. That’s good! Do you know how to, um… carry things? Because I might have just volunteered for a delivery I cannot actually lift.”
> 
> He beams, hopeful, sap-orb wobbling precariously in his hands.

## DM Notes

- He’s struggling to deliver medicinal sap-orbs to a healer.
    
- If the party helps him, he becomes instantly affectionate and follows them for a bit.
    
- If they refuse, he tries anyway, drops one, it bounces, grows into a knee-high glowing mushroom, and he panics.
    

### Skill Checks

- **DC 12 Persuasion** to calm him and get the story straight.
    
- **DC 13 Survival/Nature** to stabilize the bouncing sap-orbs.
    
- **DC 10 Insight** reveals he’s desperate for approval.
    

### Consequences

- Helping him → he calls the party “Branch-Buddies.”
    
- Ignoring him → he still secretly admires them and will reappear later.

# 🌲 Second Introduction Scene (When the Party Leaves Rootspire)

## **Read-Aloud**

> At the edge of the great root bridge, a small figure waves both arms above his head, nearly tipping over from enthusiasm.  
> “Wait! Don’t go yet! I—I made you a thing!”
> 
> He trots forward, panting, holding a crude wooden charm shaped vaguely like one of the party members.  
> “For luck,” he says. “And so you remember Rootspire. And… maybe me too?”

## DM Notes

Timoor sneaked out of the Root-Hollows without permission to give the party this charm.

### Skill Checks

- **DC 12 Insight:** He’s afraid of being forgotten.
    
- **DC 14 Nature:** The charm faintly hums with a harmonic resonance from the Veil-Seed.
    

### Consequences

- Accept the gift: Timoor blushes gold and runs off before he cries.
    
- Decline the gift: He pretends to understand, but the glow in his freckles dims.

Treat him as a **CR 1/4 fey companion** if he ever joins temporarily:

- **AC 13**, **HP 9**, **Speed 30 / Climb 20**
