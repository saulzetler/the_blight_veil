# 🌿 **1. Boughwarden Thalan – The Heartwood Warden**

### **Role**

Pragmatic leader of Rootspire. Manages safety, Council relations, and politics.

### **Read-Aloud Intro**

> A broad-shouldered elf stands before the sap-lit chamber, grey hair braided with thin root-fibers. His expression is calm but measured, as if he has already weighed your presence against Rootspire’s needs.

### **Voice & Mannerisms**

- Quiet, steady, no wasted words
    
- Hands always resting lightly on root surfaces (listening)
    
- Tilts his head slightly before speaking, as if hearing two conversations at once
    
- Speaks as though everything is a negotiation
    

### **What Thalan Knows**

- The Council of 8 is aware the party is coming
    
- The Veil tremors have increased since the Ashroot blight surged
    
- Something in the party **stirred the tree** on entry (Oscar most of all)
    
- Concord scouting forces have been seen near the lower roots
    
- Rootspire’s people are uneasy
    

### **What Thalan Wants**

- Stability
    
- Assurance that the party won’t draw Concord retaliation
    
- To confirm the party isn’t Concord or witch-sympathizers
    
- To understand why Oscar has a harmonic presence
    

### **How Thalan Reacts to PCs**

- **Oscar:** intense but polite curiosity
    
- **Zennor:** gives guarded looks at his amulet; senses discord
    
- **Fergie:** feels ember magic but doesn’t comment yet
    
- **Hog:** quietly amused by him
    

### **Secrets (require Persuasion DC 16 or deep trust)**

- He suspects one Council member wants to break from the others
    
- He believes the Trifarix has agents in Rootspire
    
- Rootspire once failed a previous trial — he is cautious
    

### **Micro-statblock**

- **AC** 13 | **HP** 40 | **WIS +5**, **CHA +3**
    
- Not meant for combat—focus on diplomacy or calling guards if attacked.

# 🪵 **2. Gatewardens Lira & Heron – Rootspire Sentries**

### **Role**

First contact. Calm, composed, protective of their city.

### **Read-Aloud**

> Two masked sentries stand beneath the natural archway, their bark-woven armor shifting like living wood. Their eyes glow faint green behind carved masks.

### **Voice & Mannerisms**

- Lira: low voice, slightly raspy
    
- Heron: soft and melodic
    
- Move in near-perfect sync
    
- Rarely reveal their faces
    
- touch roots when lying or detecting lies
    

### **They Know**

- Surface-level rebel movements
    
- Where outsiders can and can’t go
    
- Rough schedule of Council trials (not exact)
    

### **They Want**

- To keep Rootspire safe
    
- To avoid unnecessary conflict
    
- To test newcomers’ intentions
    

### **Secrets**

- They are siblings
    
- One is partially attuned to the Veil (your choice)
    

### **Checks**

- Insight (DC 12): They’re wary but curious
    
- Mentioning the Council: They become serious
    

### **Micro-statblock**

Scout equivalents but with **advantage** on Perception in Rootspire.

# 🍄 **3. Mossfire Apothecary – Nyris the Sapbrewer**

### **Role**

Potion-maker, herbalist, and quiet magical researcher.  
Sells potions and one very expensive magic item.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A wiry half-elf pulses glowing sap from a hollowed branch into small vials. His hair is tied back with moss twine, and his fingers are stained with green resin.

### **Vibe**

- Blunt, sarcastic, but not cruel
    
- Taps on glass vials while thinking
    
- Calls Oscar “Green-heart” without explanation
    

### **They Know**

- How to stabilize Veil-touched flora
    
- Rumors about the Weeping Tree
    
- Strange sigils found carved under bridges
    

### **They Want**

- Rare reagents (choose one for a quest):
    
    - Weepsap from the Weeping Tree
        
    - A blighted beetle husk
        
    - Crushed luminous moss from a distant grove
        

### **Reactions**

- **Oscar:** fascinated and confused; asks to “borrow the box” (the terrarium)
    
- **Zennor:** distrusts the amulet immediately
    
- **Fergie:** tries to sell him fire-resistant bark
    
- **Hog:** assumes Hog will break something
    

### **Secret**

He has a hidden Rootspire-only spell called **Saplight Surge** (he won’t teach it yet).

### **Micro-statblock**

Commoner with **druid cantrips** (Produce Flame, Guidance).

# 🌱 **4. Rootshaper Apprentice – Selune of the Knots**

### **Role**

Young wood-mage in training. Curious and bold.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A young woman with bark-dust on her cheeks and sleeves rolled to her elbows stares openly at Oscar’s terrarium, jaw slack. “It hummed,” she whispers.

### **Vibe**

- Energetic, scattered
    
- Talks with her hands
    
- Listens closely to the tree
    
- Prone to slipping off mid-conversation if she senses something unusual
    

### **They Know**

- How the tree reacts to outsiders
    
- That a Veil tremor recently shook the upper canopy
    
- The location of a hidden sigil chamber (good side-quest)
    

### **They Want**

- To understand the terrarium
    
- To impress the Boughwarden
    
- To figure out why Rootspire feels “awake” since the party arrived
    

### **Secrets**

- She heard the tree **whisper back** during Oscar’s entrance
    
- She thinks the party may be Veil-touched
    

### **Micro-statblock**

Druid initiate: AC 11, HP 16, **Shape Wood (cantrip-like effect)**.

# 🍃 **5. Sighing Boughs Tavern Host – Old Wren**

### **Role**

Keeper of the tavern built inside a hollow limb.  
Source of rumors and insight.

### **Read-Aloud**

> An older man with moss-grey beard wipes down a curved root counter. The entire tavern creaks softly each time the wind moves the canopy above.
> 
> “Try the elderflower ale,” he says. “It’ll either make you wise or stupid. Hard to tell until it’s too late.”

### **Vibe**

- Bitter but kind
    
- Knows too much
    
- Talks like someone who’s seen the Concord crush too many good people
    
- Dry humor
    

### **They Know**

- Every rumor in Rootspire
    
- Where the missing cobbler was last seen
    
- That someone is carving sigils into the tree at night
    

### **They Want**

- Outsiders who don’t bring trouble
    
- A missing delivery of redroot grain (small quest)
    

### **Secrets**

He fought for the Council decades ago—until something broke him.

### **Micro-statblock**

Veteran lite (AC 14 HP 44).

# 🐾 **6. Market Scout – Briar Fenstep**

### **Role**

A fast-talking, sharp-eyed local who guides newcomers.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A wiry young scout drops from an upper root with the grace of a cat. “Lost yet?” he asks with a grin. “You will be.”

### **Vibe**

- Cocky
    
- Friendly with adventurers
    
- Always “knows a shortcut”
    
- Runs small errands for coin
    

### **He Knows**

- A secret back-path to the tree’s mid-level
    
- Which areas are strictly off-limits
    
- Where Concord scouts were last sighted
    

### **He Wants**

- Coin
    
- Gossip
    
- Someone to help him retrieve a stolen pack
    

### **Secret**

He once failed the Council trial and has deep insecurity about it.

### **Micro-statblock**

Scout stats with **Advantage on Athletics & Acrobatics in Rootspire**.

# 🌾 **7. Rootspire Child – Kett (Oscar Hook)**

### **Role**

A small child who reacts instinctively to Oscar’s terrarium.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A child peers around a root-column, wide-eyed. “Mister… your treebox glows,” they whisper.

### **Vibe**

- Quiet
    
- Observant
    
- Sometimes “hears” the tree
    
- Attached to Oscar quickly
    

### **They Know**

- Innocent but sharp insights
    
- Sometimes comments on tree mood (“It’s… humming today.”)
    

### **They Want**

- Someone to listen
    
- To show a secret root-hollow where the hum is strongest
    

### **Secret**

Kett saw a Concord agent near Rootspire’s edge and is afraid to tell adults.

### **Micro-statblock**

Commoner; mechanically irrelevant but great for emotional stakes.

# 🌳 **Using These NPCs in Play**

### **How They Interact with Each Other**

- Thalan trusts Nyris for expert knowledge
    
- Selune annoys Nyris with questions
    
- Wren dislikes Briar (“too nosy”)
    
- Gatewardens begrudgingly tolerate Wren’s sarcasm
    
- Kett is known to everyone
    

### **How They Push the Party Forward**

- Gatewardens → entry & security
    
- Thalan → Council trial
    
- Selune → harmonic mysteries
    
- Nyris → potion quest & Veil lore
    
- Wren → rumors & cobbler quest
    
- Briar → fast-travel paths
    
- Kett → emotional grounding & Concord clue
