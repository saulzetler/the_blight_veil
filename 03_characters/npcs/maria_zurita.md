# **Maria Zurita — Echo of Fergie**

_A Blight Veil NPC Reference Page_  
_(Fire Genasi — Blue Flame Variant | Echo-Touched Seer of the Concord)_

---

# **Read-Aloud Introduction**

> She moves like someone who has studied every step before taking it — precise, deliberate, threaded with quiet tension. Her hair burns in cold blue flame, illuminating sharp features and eyes that glow the color of heated metal. In her palm rests a Porter Stone of pale opal, humming in perfect inversion to Fergie’s.
> 
> When she looks at him, it’s not recognition — it’s recognition of _possibility._

---

# **I. Identity & Lore**

### **Name:** Maria Zurita

### **Race:** Fire Genasi (Blue-Flame Variant)

### **Age:** 18

### **Allegiance:** Concord — Vision Branch (Seer-Acolyte)

### **Role in Campaign:**

An Echo of Fergie from another possible timeline, pulled into this reality when the Veil tore. She is the living embodiment of the path Fergie _didn’t_ choose during his Porter Stone coming-of-age ritual.

### **Appearance**

- Blue flame hair and ember-lit freckles that pulse with harmonic resonance.
    
- Clothing is Concord Vision: layered, geometric fabrics, bronze filigree, sigil plates.
    
- Blue fire flickers when she’s anxious or when Fergie is nearby.
    
- A mirrored spiral sigil appears on her skin when the Veil destabilizes.
    

### **Personality Notes**

- Ordered, internal, fearful of her own unraveling.
    
- Loyal to Concord ideals, but privately doubts she is real.
    
- Fascinated by Fergie; speaks softly to avoid disrupting their unstable resonance.
    
- Quick to anger when she feels powerless — flame intensifies cold and bright.
    

### **Core Truth**

Maria is not naturally born in this timeline.  
She is an _Echo_, a harmonic reflection of Fergie from a different reality. Her existence is conditional, and the Veil seeks resolution through either convergence or collapse.

# **II. Roleplay Guidance**

### **Voice**

- Controlled, quiet, almost meditative.
    
- Occasionally finishes Fergie’s sentences or anticipates his phrasing.
    
- When emotional, her voice harmonizes — two tones layered unnaturally.
    

### **Behavior Around Fergie**

- Mirrors his posture subconsciously.
    
- Watches him with scientific and emotional curiosity.
    
- Often pauses before speaking, as if hearing another self inside her head.
    

### **Behavior Around Others**

- Polite, reserved, slightly alien.
    
- Overly formal — Concord training.
    
- Rare but startling moments of vulnerability.
    

---

# **III. Unique Mechanics: Blue Flame Genasi**

Maria’s fire manifests as **cold blue flame**, representing her Echo nature.

### **Blue Flame Traits (Flavor + Light Mechanics)**

- **Resilience of the Echo:** Resistance to fire _and_ cold damage.
    
- **Cold Burn:** Fire-based spells deal cold damage instead, unless she chooses otherwise.
    
- **Echo Spark:** When struck to 0 HP, flickers with harmonic light — not fully dying unless the Veil severs her thread.

# **STAT BLOCK 1 — MARIA ZURITA (ACOLYTE-LEVEL)**

_CR 3 | Humanoid (Genasi), Lawful Neutral_

**Armor Class:** 15 (Concord warded vestments)  
**HP:** 45 (6d8+18)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR 10 | DEX 14 | CON 16 | INT 14 | WIS 13 | CHA 14**

**Saving Throws:** Dex +4, Con +5  
**Skills:** Insight +4, Arcana +4, Perception +3, Persuasion +4  
**Resistances:** Fire, Cold  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.  
**Languages:** Common, Ignan, Concord Cant  
**Proficiency Bonus:** +2

### **Abilities**

**Blue Flame Soul.** Her fire spells can deal cold damage instead of fire.  
**Concord Seer Training.** Advantage on Arcana checks related to sigils, harmonics, and Veil anomalies.  
**Echo-Touched.** When Fergie is within 60 ft, both gain a faint harmonic aura; once per long rest, she can read one surface emotion of his.

### **Spellcasting (3rd-level caster, CHA)**

**Spell save DC:** 12 | **Attack +4**

**Cantrips:** Produce Flame (blue), Fire Bolt (blue), Guidance  
**1st Level (4/day):** Shield, Burning Hands (blue flame → cold), Detect Magic  
**2nd Level (2/day):** Hold Person, Scorching Ray (blue flame → cold)

### **Actions**

**Blue Ember Blade.** _Melee Spell Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., 7 (1d8+3) cold or fire damage.

**Cold Burst (Recharge 5–6).** 15 ft. cone, 12 (3d6) cold damage, Dex save DC 12 for half.

# **STAT BLOCK 2 — MARIA ZURITA, ECHO-AWAKENED SEER**

_CR 7 | Humanoid (Genasi, Echo-Touched), Neutral_

**Armor Class:** 17 (Concord Seer plates)  
**HP:** 102 (12d8+48)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR 10 | DEX 16 | CON 18 | INT 16 | WIS 14 | CHA 16**

**Saving Throws:** Dex +6, Con +7, Wis +5  
**Skills:** Insight +5, Arcana +6, Investigation +6, Persuasion +6, Perception +5  
**Resistances:** Fire, Cold, Psychic  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.; **Harmonic Sight** (sees sigils and Veil fractures)  
**Languages:** Common, Ignan, Concord Cant  
**Proficiency Bonus:** +3

### **Abilities**

**Echo Convergence.**  
When within 60 ft of Fergie, both must roll a CHA save DC 13 when casting spells; on a fail, their spells harmonize, creating unpredictable effects (DM choice: empowerment, misfire, resonance burst).

**Blue Flame Mastery.**  
All fire spells may convert to cold. When she converts, add +2 damage per spell level.

**Fracture-Born.**  
Once per long rest, when she drops to 0 HP, she instead becomes ethereal until the start of her next turn and regains 10 HP.

**Veil Sense.**  
Automatically detects nearby Veil lesions, illusions, or temporal distortions.

### **Spellcasting (7th-level caster, CHA)**

**Spell save DC:** 14 | **Spell attack +6**

**Cantrips:** Fire Bolt (blue), Control Flames, Thaumaturgy, Guidance

**1st level (4 slots):** Shield, Burning Hands, Detect Magic  
**2nd level (3 slots):** Hold Person, Scorching Ray, Mirror Image  
**3rd level (3 slots):** Counterspell, Fireball (blue → cold), Hypnotic Pattern  
**4th level (1 slot):** Ice Storm (flavor: blue flame hail)

### **Actions**

**Blue Flame Rapier.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, 1d8+3 cold.

**Harmonic Pulse (Recharge 5–6).**  
20-ft radius burst of Veil energy.  
Creatures must make DC 15 Con save or take 4d8 thunder + 2d6 psychic; half on success.

# **V. Maria’s Equipment**

### **Opal Porter Stone (Inverted Resonance)**

- Hums in mirror frequency to Fergie’s Stone.
    
- When brought together, both Stones destabilize (DM can trigger visions, identity bleed, or harmonic anomalies).
    
- Glows brightest when a Veil tear is near.
    

### **Concord Vision Armor**

Bronze geometric plating, light sigils on the inside. +2 AC, advantage vs illusions.

# **VI. Plot Hooks & Moments to Drop In**

### **1. The Mirrored Choice**

She shares a memory of her Porter Stone ritual — the moment she “chose duty.”  
This should echo something Fergie has not shared with the party yet.

### **2. The Harmonic Collapse**

Their Stones resonate uncontrollably; both see visions of a fused identity.

### **3. Maria’s Doubt**

She confesses she fears she is a construct — or worse, an intrusion.

### **4. Maria’s Hidden Orders**

The Concord instructs her:  
**“If the Echo cannot be stabilized, it must be severed.”**
