# ⚔️ **Ashroot Rebel Camp – Combat Contingency**

### Encounter Summary

- **Difficulty:** Hard (3 NPCs with good coordination and environment advantage)
    
- **Environment:** Riverbank with light cover (fallen logs, tents, rocks).
    
- **Tactics:** Rilwen keeps range and uses terrain; Cadran holds the front line; Iraen heals and controls with nature magic.
    
- **Morale:** They fight to defend themselves but retreat or surrender if reduced below half HP total.
    

---

## 🏹 **Rilwen Thorne – Ashroot Scout**

_Medium humanoid (half-elf), neutral good_  
**Armor Class:** 14 (studded leather)  
**Hit Points:** 42 (8d8 + 8)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 11 (+0) | **DEX** 16 (+3) | **CON** 12 (+1) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

**Saving Throws:** Dex +5, Wis +4  
**Skills:** Stealth +7, Perception +4, Survival +4  
**Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14  
**Languages:** Common, Elvish  
**Challenge:** 2 (450 XP)

### Traits

- **Woodland Ambusher.** On the first round of combat, Rilwen has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn’t acted yet.
    
- **Cunning Step.** She can take the Hide or Disengage action as a bonus action.
    

### Actions

- **Multiattack.** Two shortbow or shortsword attacks.
    
- **Shortbow.** +5 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.
    
- **Shortsword.** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., _Hit:_ 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.
    

### Special Actions

- **Shadow Volley (Recharge 5–6).** Fires a quick spread of two arrows at once. Each creature of her choice within a 10-ft. radius of a point she can see must make a DC 13 Dex save or take 9 (2d8) piercing damage.
    
- **Signal Flare (1/day).** Fires a crackling green flare into the air, summoning nearby rebels or startling beasts. If used, roll a d4 — on a 4, one **Ashroot Hunter** (scout stat block) arrives in 1d4 rounds.

## 🪓 **Cadran Holt – Veteran Turncoat**

_Medium humanoid (human), chaotic good_  
**Armor Class:** 16 (chain shirt)  
**Hit Points:** 55 (10d8 + 10)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 15 (+2) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 12 (+1) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 13 (+1) | **CHA** 12 (+1)

**Saving Throws:** Str +4, Con +3  
**Skills:** Athletics +4, Intimidation +3  
**Senses:** passive Perception 11  
**Languages:** Common  
**Challenge:** 3 (700 XP)

### Traits

- **Seasoned Fighter.** When he takes damage from a weapon attack, he can use his reaction to reduce it by 5 (once per turn).
    
- **Grit and Ash.** Advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
    

### Actions

- **Multiattack.** Makes two melee attacks.
    
- **Handaxe.** +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., _Hit:_ 7 (1d10 + 2) slashing damage.
    
- **Throwing Knife.** +4 to hit, range 20/60 ft., _Hit:_ 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.
    

### Special Actions

- **Crippling Strike (Recharge 5–6).** Cadran makes a single powerful blow. On hit, target must succeed on a DC 13 Con save or have its speed halved until the end of its next turn.
    
- **Battle Roar (1/day).** Lets out a booming cry — allies within 30 ft. gain advantage on their next attack roll before the start of his next turn.

## 🌿 **Iraen Brook – River Healer**

_Medium humanoid (human druid), neutral good_  
**Armor Class:** 13 (leather armor)  
**Hit Points:** 38 (7d8 + 7)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 12 (+1) | **CON** 12 (+1) | **INT** 10 (+0) | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 13 (+1)

**Saving Throws:** Int +2, Wis +5  
**Skills:** Medicine +5, Nature +4, Insight +5  
**Senses:** passive Perception 13  
**Languages:** Common, Druidic  
**Challenge:** 2 (450 XP)

### Traits

- **River’s Blessing.** When standing within 10 ft. of running water, Iraen can use a bonus action to heal 1d4 HP.
    
- **Spellcasting.** Iraen is a 5th-level spellcaster (Wisdom +5, DC 13).  
    **Prepared Spells (slots 4/3/2):**
    
    - _Cantrips:_ Guidance, Produce Flame, Thorn Whip
        
    - _1st:_ Cure Wounds, Entangle, Faerie Fire
        
    - _2nd:_ Lesser Restoration, Flaming Sphere
        
    - _3rd:_ Call Lightning
        

### Actions

- **Quarterstaff.** +2 to hit, 1d6 bludgeoning.
    
- **Produce Flame.** +5 to hit, 2d8 fire damage.
    

### Special Actions

- **Call of the River (Recharge 6).** Summons a surge of spectral water in a 10-ft. radius. Enemies in the area must make a DC 13 Str save or be knocked prone and take 8 (2d8) bludgeoning damage.
    
- **Healing Surge (1/day).** As an action, channels water energy to heal all allies within 20 ft. for 8 (2d8) HP.

## 🪓 Encounter Notes for DM

- **If they attack first:** Rilwen and Iraen will stay behind partial cover, firing and casting from range, while Cadran engages in melee.
    
- **Terrain advantage:** Give the rebels +2 AC from half-cover if they fight from behind their camp setup or trees.
    
- **Retreat trigger:** If Iraen is downed or two rebels are below 10 HP, they flee into the forest — possibly creating a pursuit or moral dilemma.
