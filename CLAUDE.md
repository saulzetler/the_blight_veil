## World Essentials

- **The Concord** — Authoritarian ruling body. Controls the known world through three arms: **Might**, **Vision**, and **Guile** (the Trifarix).
- **Orrenval** — Capital city. Contains the **Sanctara**, which secretly houses the **Veil Core**.
- **The Ashroot** — Forest region outside Concord control. Contains **Rootspire** (The Great Tree City).
- **The Veil** — A membrane between the mortal world and buried divinity, born from the **Godfall**. Alive but not conscious. Tries to reassemble the divine. Not evil — just hungry.
- **The Veil Core** — A crystalline sphere beneath the Sanctara. It IS a god (or its last fragment). The Concord secretly powers the Barrier of Dawn by feeding it mortal fear, faith, and death.

## Key Secrets (DM Only)
- The Barrier of Dawn is a **vacuum**, not a shield — it sustains itself by consuming vitality from the land and people.
- The Trifarix's rites literally harvest dying fear and emotion to fuel the Core.
- **Project Dawnswell** — Guile's secret plan to replicate the Core; they believe the original is dying.
- The **Choir of Binding** — 13 priests who maintain the Core directly; they slowly petrify into crystal as it consumes them.
- The **Ashroot Resonance** (current campaign time) — the Ashroot may be growing a second Core, or the Veil is learning to harmonize.

## Tone & Style

Dark, mythic, melancholic. Beauty buried in decay. The world is not black-and-white — the Concord genuinely believes it is saving civilization. The Veil's goal is creation, not destruction. Revelations should feel earned.
