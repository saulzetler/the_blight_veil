    Region Questlines

    🕯️ 1. The Etherlight Concord¶

    Core Themes: Theocratic control, moral purges, elite hypocrisy

    Overview:¶

    The spiritual and political heart of the empire. Gleaming cities, propaganda-filled sermons, and inquisitorial spies in every crowd. “Purity” is law, and “heresy” is defined by whoever holds the most power.

    Quest: "The Choir of Ashes"¶

    Start: A child sings a forbidden melody in a plaza. The crowd panics. Inquisitors seize the child. Investigation: The players learn the tune is from an old hymn that predates the Concord but is now banned as heretical. Following leads to a hidden network of musicians called the Cindersong, who preserve lost cultural heritage through coded songs.

    Choices:

    • Join the Cindersong and help them spread their message through underground performances.
    • Expose them to gain favor with the Concord — but find the inquisitors using the lyrics for torture.
    • Reveal the truth to the public: the song is Concord's own rewritten history, purged for political reasons.

    🌿 2. Ashroot¶

    Core Themes: Cultural resilience, forbidden rites, nature vs. civilization

    Overview:¶

    Dense forests, sacred groves, and hidden villages where ancestral rites persist. Viewed by the Concord as "uncivilized" and "corrupted." A land of secret pacts and blood-stained memory.

    Quest: "The Wound in the Wood"¶

    Start: A tree in a sacred grove bleeds real blood after a Concord missionary desecrates it. Spirits grow restless. Locals blame each other. Investigation: The tree was a seal binding an old nature-spirit, wounded by colonial magic. A warband of Ashroot shamans wants to awaken it to fight back.

    Choices:

    • Help awaken the spirit and unleash it — potentially powerful but uncontrollable.
    • Rebind it, preserving balance but leaving the land vulnerable to more Concord raids.
    • Seek a third path — use arcane knowledge or diplomacy to heal the seal and forge a new pact.

    ⛰️ 3. The Godscar¶

    Core Themes: War trauma, blighted landscapes, broken divinity

    Overview:¶

    Once a holy land — now shattered by divine warfare and forbidden experiments. The soil is cursed, the air buzzes with residual divine energy. Ruins of lost civilizations dot the region.

    Quest: "Shadows of the Shell-God"¶

    Start: A refugee camp is being haunted by visions of a massive, limbless divine corpse buried beneath the hills. Investigation: The players can follow trails to a massive crater where a god once fell. Artifacts nearby grant miracles — or madness.

    Choices:

    • Harvest godflesh for powerful but unstable magical items.
    • Try to cleanse the land, but doing so destroys the artifacts — and may awaken other dangerous remnants.
    • Offer the site to the Godscar cult, who want to restore divine war through sacrifice.

    🌊 4. Ibaros & Corsaire Sound¶

    Core Themes: Rebellion, pirate democracy, Concord subterfuge

    Overview:¶

    An archipelago that resisted colonization the longest. Now it's a pirate haven, trade hub, and a brewing storm of political upheaval. Smugglers ferry forbidden texts and stolen relics.

    Quest: "The Salt-Born Pact"¶

    Start: A pirate queen offers the players a rare artifact if they sabotage a Concord naval blockade. Investigation: The artifact is a relic of the sea god once worshipped across the islands — now outlawed. Her crew is becoming fanatical, influenced by dreams from the deep.

    Choices:

    • Aid the blockade sabotage, empowering a rebellion that could spiral into religious fanaticism.
    • Steal the artifact and sell it — but suffer consequences as the sea god marks them.
    • Warn the Concord, but discover they’re conducting rituals against the same god to control weather.

    🔥 5. Zathira¶

    Core Themes: Cultural melting pot, espionage, buried magic

    Overview:¶

    A frontier city-state recently absorbed by the Concord but still full of independent thinkers, rogue mages, and foreign traders. A powder keg of ideological tension.

    Quest: "The Mask of Three Faces"¶

    Start: A Concord official is assassinated. The killers leave behind an ancient mask marked with three interlocking symbols. Investigation: The mask is tied to an old trinity cult (mind, flesh, and will) that once ruled Zathira in secret. Their surviving agents are plotting a return.

    Choices:

    • Infiltrate the cult and take control of its network.
    • Expose it to the Concord — who decide to raze a whole district in retaliation.
    • Play both sides, manipulating Concord paranoia and cult ambitions for the players’ own ends.

    🩸 6. The Riven Marches¶

    Core Themes: Border conflict, scorched earth, local resistance

    Overview:¶

    The contested buffer between Ashroot and the Concord. War-torn villages, famine, and shifting allegiances. Every faction has spies here.

    Quest: "Ashes for Bread"¶

    Start: A village pleads for food aid from both Concord forces and Ashroot guerrillas. Each offers safety in exchange for loyalty. Investigation: The land is dying because a Concord general cursed the soil to force surrender years ago — the effects persist.

    Choices:

    • Side with Concord, restore supplies, but enforce harsh loyalty and convert villagers.
    • Aid Ashroot fighters, but risk reprisals and executions.
    • Use forgotten nature magic to purify the land, turning the village into a neutral sanctuary — if the players can protect it.
