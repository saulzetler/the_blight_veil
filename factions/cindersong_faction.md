# **The Cindersong**

**Overview:**  
The Cindersong is a secretive network of musicians, rebels, and heretics who preserve forbidden hymns and lost cultural heritage. Their coded songs carry messages of hope, resistance, and hidden truths, echoing through the underbelly of Concord society. To the authorities, they are dangerous agitators; to the oppressed, they are a lifeline and a spark of rebellion.

---

## **Origins & Beliefs**
Born from the ashes of the Godfall and Concord purges, the Cindersong formed to protect melodies and stories deemed heretical. They believe music and memory can heal the wounds of the Blight Veil and awaken the world to its true history. Their hymns are said to carry divine resonance, shifting reality and inspiring courage.

---

## **Leadership & Structure**
The Cindersong is decentralized, led by a council of hidden conductors and influential performers. Notable members include:
- **Marla Veinwright:** Defiant heretic, symbol of resistance, whose song sparked public unrest.
- **The Whisperer:** An anonymous figure who coordinates coded messages and safehouses.

---

## **Principles & Allegiances**
- Preserve forbidden knowledge and culture.
- Oppose Concord oppression and censorship.
- Aid the Blighted, exiles, and other marginalized groups.
- Forge alliances with Ashroot shamans, Free Cities traders, and sympathetic Concord officials.

---

## **Notable Events**
- The Choir of Ashes: A public performance that led to mass arrests and the spread of Cindersong influence.
- The Rescue of Marla: A pivotal moment where the party may intervene, shaping the faction’s future.
- Infiltration of Concord: Cindersong sympathizers have reached even the capital’s heart, as seen in session 1.

---

## **Role in the Campaign**
The Cindersong offers quests, secret knowledge, and moral dilemmas. Their actions can sway public opinion, incite rebellion, or reveal hidden truths about the Blight Veil and the Concord’s history.
