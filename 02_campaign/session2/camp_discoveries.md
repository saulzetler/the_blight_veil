# 🕯️ Ashroot Rebel Camp — Information and Clues

This camp represents the **first true contact with the Ashroot resistance**, and it can bridge your early escape scenes into the broader forest narrative. Every piece of intel below either connects to existing lore or foreshadows quests in _Ashroot_, _the Riven Marches_, or _the Godscar._

---

## 🎯 1. General Camp Investigation

| Action                                  | Check                               | Result                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Search the camp supplies**            | **Investigation DC 12**             | Players find salvaged Concord rations and a ledger stamped with a faded military seal. It mentions “**patrols diverted from the Riven Marches**” — implying Concord’s border is stretched thin after uprisings there. [[region_questlines]]                    |
| **Inspect the maps and scouting notes** | **Survival or Investigation DC 14** | The rebels are marking “blight-patch” movements near the old **Woundwood Hollow** (a future encounter tied to _The Wound in the Wood_ quest). Rilwen explains they’ve seen animals behaving strangely — walking in circles, whispering.                        |
| **Examine their weaponry**              | **Arcana or Nature DC 13**          | Some arrowheads are coated with a sticky black resin — a crude **anti-blight mixture** made from river-herbs and ashroot sap. It weakens Veil-touched creatures (disadvantage on their next Con save if hit). If players ask, Iraen can teach them the recipe. |

## 🗣️ 2. Dialog-Based Intel from NPCs

Each NPC will share information only if the players gain trust (mentioning opposition to the Concord or killing a captain).

### **Rilwen Thorne — The Scout**

- **Prompted by:** Persuasion DC 14 or open hostility toward the Concord.
    
- **Intel:**
    
    - Mentions the **Trifarix’s internal conflict** — rumors that _Varric Thane_ and _Elira Voss_ are feuding over “who counts as pure.” This matches the current schism in the Concord hierarchy. [[concord_faction]]
        
    - Knows that Concord inquisitors recently executed an entire patrol for “listening to false hymns,” suggesting paranoia spreading through the ranks.
        
    - If asked about Iria Cael, she calls her “the silver-tongued field angel” and says the Ashroot rebels still debate whether she’s truly free of the Concord’s influence.
        

### **Cadran Holt — The Veteran**

- **Prompted by:** Insightful empathy (Persuasion DC 13) or shared ex-military experience.
    
- **Intel:**
    
    - Confirms he once served under **Varric Thane** at the **Siege of Aster’s Hollow** and saw him burn civilians alive to stop “blight contagion”. [[concord_faction]]
        
    - Claims that some soldiers now secretly worship **Esmir the Hollow**, believing “blight brings freedom”. [[blighted_faction]]
        
    - Warns that “someone called **Corvane** is buying silence” — a reference to _Lord Silven Corvane_, the Pillar of Guile. If pressed, he suspects Corvane funds both sides of the war for profit. 
        
    - Hints that the Concord’s lines around **the Riven Marches** are weakening due to food shortages and deserters. [[region_questlines]]
        

### **Iraen Brook — The Healer**

- **Prompted by:** Calm, respectful tone or mention of nature/faith.
    
- **Intel:**
    
    - Whispers about **Veil Anomalies** deeper in the forest — “places where the dead talk in the trees”. [[expanded_lore]]
        
    - Speaks of an **artifact called the Ashroot Seed**, a relic said to restore corrupted land. He doesn’t know where it is, only that shamans once guarded it beneath a bleeding tree. [[expanded_lore]]
        
    - Mentions that Concord missionaries once tried to purify the river — it ran red for a week afterward, and the spirits have been restless since. 
        
    - If comforted with a gentle approach (Persuasion DC 15), he admits his healing magic comes from “Veil-kissed water,” suggesting the Ashroot rebels quietly use blighted energy rather than reject it.

## 🔍 3. Passive Lore Opportunities

|Observation|Passive DC|Result|
|---|---|---|
|**Strange markings on tree bark**|12|The symbols are **pre-Concord druidic wards** from the time before the Godfall. Knowledge (History or Arcana DC 14) reveals they were meant to repel divine fire — proof the Ashroot still preserve ancient rites.|
|**The rebels’ campfire song**|10|The melody echoes fragments of an old **Cindersong hymn**, connecting back to the forbidden music network. A bard or performer might recognize the tune as centuries old, once sung in the Sanctara.|
|**Weathered wooden idol by the fire**|11|A tiny effigy of a _stag-headed spirit_—symbol of the **old Ashroot tribes** burned during the Concord expansion. If a player touches it with reverence, they feel a faint pulse of warmth (harmless).|

## 🗺️ 5. Intel Summary for Players

|Category|Key Takeaway|
|---|---|
|**The Concord**|The Trifarix is divided; paranoia and purges spreading; soldiers deserting.|
|**The Blighted**|Rumors of Esmir’s followers defecting from Concord ranks; the Veil may be spreading faster than officially stated.|
|**The Ashroot**|Deep forest contains anomalies and ancient seals (possible hook to _The Wound in the Wood_). A council upriver (“The Circle’s Root”) is organizing resistance.|
|**Artifacts & Mysteries**|Mentions of the **Ashroot Seed**, the **Lantern of Dawn**, and the **bleeding tree** — all potential questline anchors.|
|**Tone for Next Act**|Hopeful but heavy — the sense that rebellion and corruption are intertwined; nothing pure survives untouched.|
