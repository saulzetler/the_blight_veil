# **Session Recap – Into the Ashroot (Nov 7)**

After a tense morning on the road, the party finally compared the strange trinkets they’d each carried from their pasts.  
**Fergie’s porter stone**, a black opal from his coming-of-age rite, had glowed when the Veil tore.  
**Hog’s tile**, marked with a gauntleted fist wreathed in lightning, had sparked the same way.  
Even **Zennor’s bracelet**, a keepsake from his murdered daughter, seemed tied to whatever forces now roil across the land.

### **The Forgotten Grove & the Monolith**

The group stumbled into a quiet grove where a tall, weathered stone monolith stood half-sunken in the earth.  
A faint hum vibrated beneath it. A Cindersong sigil was etched on its face.

Experimenting with flute notes and humming, the party eventually found the right **three-part harmony** — unlocking a hidden chamber beneath the stone.

Inside were **three relics**:

- **Emberheart Pendant** — now carried by Fergie
    
- **Cindersong Blade** — expertly claimed by Zennor
    
- **Song of the Veiled Choir** — a silence-weaving scroll
    

### **Signs of Faith and Rot**

Continuing toward the Ashroot, the party found a **defiled shrine to Lathander**.  
Fergie restored it, saying a quiet prayer.

Oscar then spotted a **Concord insignia** hanging from a tree (buried, then immediately unburied again) and later discovered a piece of **rusted Concord armor** that resembled parts of his own frame — a disturbing echo of his origins.

### **Rebel Contact**

A scout ambushed the group, demanding to know where they stood.  
After loudly denouncing the Concord (and claiming Captain Deyran’s death), they were brought to a hidden rebel camp and met:

- **Rilwen Thorn** — half-elf scout, scarred and sharp
    
- **Cadran Holt** — grizzled veteran with a wooden prosthetic
    
- **Iraen Brook** — soft-spoken young druid with a glowing riverstone
    

Zennor and Cadran immediately bonded over being gruff warrior men.

From the rebels, the party learned about the Concord’s ruling **Trifarix**:  
**Varric Thane (Might), Elira Voss (Vision), Silven Corvane (Guile)** — and rumors of rising tension between Thane and Voss over questions of “purity.”

The rebels also spoke of the **Council of Eight**, rulers of the Ashroot.  
Oscar found a **rough forest map**, noting that Concord patrols never enter the **Riven Marches** — a long-scarred battleground neither side claims.

### **Nightfall & the Concord Assault**

As the camp settled down for the night, a blazing white light flooded through the trees:

**“By order of the Concord — lay down your arms and submit to the Light!”**

A Concord patrol attacked with lanterns that burned the earth itself.

During the fight:

- The patrol leader shattered a vial of **purple ichor**, mutating into a hulking brute.
    
- Zennor was knocked unconscious — twice — but his daughter’s bracelet and a perfectly pitched hum saved his life both times.
    
- The rebels fought fiercely at the party’s side.
    

At last, the mutated officer fell. The clearing dimmed.  
The rebels and party shared a moment of exhausted triumph… and the night finally settled in around them.
