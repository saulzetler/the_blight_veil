## 🏕️ Scene: The Riverbank Camp

Song: Ethreal Cinematic Ambient

### 🎭 **Read-Aloud Description**

> The river finds them before the people do — a slow, silvery thread winding through the mist and stone. The air is damp and smells of pine-sap and smoke.
> 
> Across the water, faint light flickers between the trees: a small campfire, half-hidden behind brush. Shapes move in the glow — three, maybe four figures — quiet and watchful.
> 
> The nearer you step, the clearer the scene becomes: a simple camp of worn bedrolls and bundled packs, drying herbs hung from a line, a makeshift spit turning a small hare over the flames. No banners, no Concord insignia. Just tired people surviving the night.
> 
> One of them spots you, rises with bow in hand — but not drawn — and calls across the river, her voice low and wary:  
> “If you’re Concord, you die here. If you’re not... cross slow, and keep your hands where I can see them.”

🕯️ _Tone note for DM:_ Deliver this with a hush — it’s not a confrontation, it’s tension born from exhaustion. Let players decide how to reveal who they are and whether to mention killing the Concord captain. Once trust is gained, the atmosphere should soften into warmth and relief.

---

## 👥 **NPCs of the Camp**

### 1. **Rilwen Thorne – The Scout**

- **Appearance:** Lean half-elf woman with mud-streaked armor and a dark green cloak; one eye clouded white from an old burn.
    
- **Personality:** Wary, sardonic, protective of her group but softens quickly when she believes someone’s fought the Concord.
    
- **Voice cues:** Low, curt, practical; she speaks like someone used to whispering in the dark.
    
- **Hooks / Dialogue seeds:**
    
    - “The Concord calls this forest cursed. That’s how they justify burning it.”
        
    - “You killed a captain? Good. That’s one less beast in their gilded cage.”
        
    - “If you’re heading deeper in, keep to the moss-side of the trees — the black roots still hum with their witch-fire.”
        
- **Purpose:** The “gatekeeper” of trust; the one to invite them into the fire once convinced.
    

---

### 2. **Cadran Holt – The Old Rebel**

- **Appearance:** Broad-shouldered human man in his fifties, greying beard, one arm replaced with a crude wooden prosthetic bound in rope and brass nails.
    
- **Personality:** Gruff but kind; a father-figure to younger rebels. Tends the cooking pot, hums old military hymns turned into drinking songs.
    
- **Voice cues:** Deep, slow, with an Ashroot drawl.
    
- **Hooks / Dialogue seeds:**
    
    - “I was Concord once. Thought I was serving the light. Turns out I was just feeding their fire.”
        
    - “They say the Trifarix don’t bleed. That they share their veins with angels. Lies, maybe — but it’s a story that keeps the peasants quiet.”
        
    - “You got the look of people who ain’t sure what side they’re on yet. Don’t wait too long to choose.”
        
- **Purpose:** Delivers moral grounding and hints of the Trifarix’s mythic reputation.
    

---

### 3. **Iraen Brook – The Healer**

- **Appearance:** Pale, freckled young man with moss-stained robes, bare feet, and a faint green light occasionally pulsing from the riverstone amulet at his neck.
    
- **Personality:** Gentle, almost ethereal; speaks softly, believes in mercy even for the damned.
    
- **Voice cues:** Calm and melodic.
    
- **Hooks / Dialogue seeds:**
    
    - “The river carries memory here. Don’t drink from it after moonrise — it shows you things you’re not ready to see.”
        
    - “If you walk with Iria Cael, you’ve already met someone braver than most of us.”
        
    - “The Ashroot still remembers what it was before the Concord. If you listen, you might too.”
        
- **Purpose:** Adds mystic flavor and helps bridge the tone toward more ancient, magical themes within the Ashroot.
    

---

## 🔥 **Campfire Moment – Player Roleplay Prompts**

Once trust is earned, let the party settle around the fire. Use these light RP cues to draw them in:

- **Cadran:** “You’ve all seen it, haven’t you? The Concord’s mercy. Tell me — what made you finally turn your backs on them?”
    
- **Rilwen:** “If you could strike at the Trifarix themselves… which one would you start with?” _(lets players express anger or ideology)_
    
- **Iraen:** “Everyone carries a wound into this forest. Sometimes it’s better to let it speak.” _(an opening for personal backstory reflection)_
    
- Between NPC exchanges, add comforting sounds — crackling fire, insects, river murmuring — to let the silence between words feel safe rather than empty.
    

---

## 🪶 **Lore & Forward Hooks**

The NPCs can drop hints that foreshadow the next act:

- **Rilwen:** Mentions a rebel stronghold “two days upriver” where the _Ashroot Circle_ gathers to plan raids.
    
- **Cadran:** Mentions hearing of “a Concord relic buried beneath a fallen chapel” — possibly a quest hook.
    
- **Iraen:** Warns that the Veil “moves with the wind,” and that some say the _Ashroot itself is learning to fight back._
