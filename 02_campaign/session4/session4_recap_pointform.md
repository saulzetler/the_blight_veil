# The Blight Veil — Session 4 Recap (Point Form)

**Party:** Zennor (Kyle) · Hog Thunderwood (Dawson) · Fergie (Paul) · Oscar / 05C4R

---

## The Council

- Party solved the glyph puzzle using information recalled from the Session 3 trial
- After some hesitation, the party entered the portal one by one
- Inside the chamber, the Council delivered the mandate: travel to Veil anomaly sites, observe, do not attempt repair, return with patterns
- When asked what to do at the anomalies, The Whispering Mask blurted _"PURIFY THEM."_
- The Veil Watcher immediately silenced the Mask: _"You are on this council. But you are not granted the power to speak."_
- The Council gave the party the Anomaly Reliquary before dismissing them

## Jaihroquai

- Fergie attuned to the Anomaly Reliquary
- During attunement, the relic revealed its name: Jaihroquai
- Jaihroquai guides the attuned toward the nearest Veil anomaly
- Jaihroquai does not care about Hog
- Hog desperately wants Jaihroquai's approval

## The Split Bark Tavern

- Party visited The Split Bark tavern in Rootspire
- Hog and Zennor fought in the chalk circle arena
- Both ordered two Moss-Foam Lagers
- Party long-rested at the tavern

## Timoor

- Party encountered Timoor outside the tavern
- Someone misheard his name as Tumor
- Party attempted to interrogate Timoor to assess whether he was dangerous
- Questions included asking his favourite colour; one contribution was an instruction to punch Hog in the face
- Threat level: unresolved
- Hog and Timoor skipped off together merrily
- Party asked Timoor to join them going forward

## The Notice Board

- Jaihroquai was pointing west toward the Spiral Loom
- Party found the Notice Board on the way
- Noticed a posting about an unidentified construct seen in the lower spiral emitting geometric light
- There were also other messages on the notice board:
	- **"Heat Without Flame"** — Seeking info on controlled flame events near lower eastern roots. No embers found. Witness reports describe a woman walking through smoke untouched.
	- **"Champion's Return?"** — Orrenval's underground fighting circuit rumored to reopen. Fighters seeking rematches.
	- **"Witchlight Sightings"** — Reports of a woman cloaked in lantern-glow along the riverbank. Payment for confirmation.
	- **"Root Rot Below"** — Sap collection crews refusing lower tunnel shifts. Complaints of humming in the dark
- Party decided to investigate the lower roots based on this posting

## The Messenger

- A rebel runner arrived — breathless, two riders lost on the road
- Carried a cracked-seal Concord message cylinder
- Message: three Orrenval districts in protest following Captain Deyran's execution, purity enforcement intensified, more unrest expected
- A second line in different ink: _"The fire sings louder than they can silence."_
- Party engaged with lots of questions
- The Trifarix is not unified in its response to the unrest
