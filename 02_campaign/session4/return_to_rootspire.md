# Session: Return to Rootspire

## Scene Structure Overview

After fallout, the party re-enters Rootspire.

They can:

1. Go to the **Tavern** (arena game + drinks + rumors)
    
2. Explore the **Market** (meet Timoor)
    
3. Visit the **Spiral Loom** (new location)
    
4. Check the **Notice Board** (quest hooks)
    

Let them choose.

---

# Read-Aloud: Rootspire Re-Entry

> The Great Tree does not pause for revelation.
> 
> Rootspire rises in living spirals above you — bark woven with lanternlight, rope bridges swaying gently in the afternoon air.
> 
> The scent of sap and cooking oil mixes with damp moss.
> 
> People move. Trade continues. Laughter drifts from somewhere below.
> 
> Whatever the council knows — this place does not.

---

## Timoor Introduction

He should:

- Notice the Reliquary immediately.
    
- Tilt his head too far to one side.
    
- Speak in metaphors about “thin bark” and “roots drinking wrong water.”
    

Suggested first line:

> “You smell like places that shouldn’t open.”

---

## What Timoor Knows

- The Veil feels _closer to the roots lately_.
    
- Something beneath Rootspire hums incorrectly.
    
- “Old tunnels” have gone quiet.
    

He does NOT explain directly.

---

## Skill Checks

**Insight DC 14**

- He is not hostile.
    
- He is curious — and cautious.
    

**Nature DC 13**

- The moss near him is subtly reactive to the Reliquary.
    

---

## Consequence

If they engage him:

- He may offer to “walk the lower spirals” later.
    
- He becomes a recurring guide.

# The Arena Game

Use your framework from [[arena_game]].

Keep it:

- Fast
    
- Low lethality
    
- Social
    

Mechanic suggestion:

- Two players enter circle.
    
- 3 rounds.
    
- Skill-based (Athletics, Acrobatics, Deception, Performance).
    
- Crowd betting influences DCs.
    

Winner gains:

- 1 gp pot
    
- Tavern reputation
    
- Rumor access
    

This is a social glue moment.

- Foreshadow anomaly under Rootspire.
    

---

## Read-Aloud

> The Spiral Loom is quieter than the market.
> 
> Workers press heated sap into carved channels, reinforcing load-bearing roots.
> 
> One section, however, is cordoned off.
> 
> The wood there pulses faintly.
> 
> As if breathing.

---

## What’s Happening

- A structural team reports “hollow resonance.”
    
- The Reliquary hums softly here.
    
- No visible tear — just wrongness.
    

This seeds a future anomaly.
