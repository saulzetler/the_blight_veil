## 🎙 **Session 1 Recap — “The Veil Remembers”**

_The bells of the Sanctara still toll in your memory…_

In the heart of **Orrenval**, beneath the Concord’s gilded banners, the people gathered for another public trial.  
A woman named **Marla** stood accused of heresy — her only crime, a forbidden song.  
But when the Inquisitors called for fire and blade, four strangers stepped forward.

**Ashton Zennor**, a weathered soldier with blood on his conscience.  
**Fergie**, a zealot of the Radiant Dawn 
**Hog**, the dwarf with a quick temper
And **Oscar** — the nameless, newly awakened construct, unsure of how long he’d slept or why he was made at all.

Steel clashed in the square as the trial descended into chaos.  
Captain **Deyran** fell beneath holy light, and the crowd scattered in terror.  
Amid the smoke and cries, a **shadowed figure** beckoned the fugitives through hidden alleys — into the arms of the **Cindersong**, a secretive resistance sworn against the Concord.

There, in the glow of guttering candles, **Vaereth** offered them refuge and a choice of escape.  
The party chose the **east**, toward the **Ashroot** — a forest whispered to shelter both rebels and blight.  
They rested in hiding while the city hunted for them.

At dawn they fled.  
Through empty streets and sleeping wards, they cut down the last guards at Orrenval’s gate and vanished beyond the walls.  
But as they ran, the world itself seemed to shudder.

A **thin tear** split the air — a seam of light and shadow.  
From within, a **veiled silhouette** reached toward them, whispering words that seared their minds:

> “The veil remembers what it was made to forget.”

Then came silence — and darkness, as every torch in Orrenval was snuffed out.

The bells rang again.

And somewhere beyond the horizon, the **Ashroot** waited.
