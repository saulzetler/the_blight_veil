# Session 1 NPC Reactions to Player Characters

## Marla Veinwright Interacts with Fergie
> "You carry the fire of Emberhold, don’t you? I’ve heard the legends — the child who sparked the sky. Tell me, do you still hear the song beneath the ashlands? If you do, don’t let the Concord silence it."

## Inquisitor Rellis Interacts with Hog
> "Thunderwood, is it? I’ve read the reports. The arena, the brawls, the bottomless trousers. You bring chaos wherever you go. But tell me, Hog — do you fight for glory, or for something deeper? The Concord has little patience for legends."

## Captain Deyran Interacts with Oscar (Verdan)
> "A construct from Lumestra? I thought your kind were meant to heal, not meddle in heresy. Are you here to judge us, machine? Or do you seek to spread your roots in forbidden soil? The Concord will not tolerate unpredictable growth."

## Sympathetic Citizen (Cindersong) to Fergie
> "You’re the Emberhold smith’s child, aren’t you? My cousin saw you at the forging festival — said your flames danced with the hymn. If you stand with Marla, maybe there’s hope for us yet."

## Fearful Citizen to Hog
> "Wait, you’re Hog the Bottomless? I saw you fight in the tavern! Please, don’t start anything here — the Concord is watching."

## Marla Veinwright to Oscar (Verdan)
> "Verdancy Unit… I’ve heard of your kind. Tessaline Marr’s work, yes? If you can change, if you can choose, then maybe you’re more alive than the laws that bind us. Will you help us grow something new in this dying city?"
