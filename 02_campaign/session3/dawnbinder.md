# ☀️ **Dawnbinder Visions — Player-Specific**

# **🌩️ HOG THUNDERWOOD — Echo of the Stormfather**

### **Read-Aloud**

> A golden light trembles, shifting into a storm-lit sky.  
> A mountain ridge rises beneath you, split by a jagged vein of lightning that pulses with your heartbeat.

### **Vision**

Hog stands on a high summit. Wind coils into a faint silhouette — a towering figure of mist and crackling stormlight. A distant echo of the Stormfather… or perhaps a reflection of what Hog could become.

Two paths appear:

1. **Path of Roaring Storms** – violent, brilliant, uncontrolled. Power without purpose.
    
2. **Path of Gentle Rain** – quieter, softer, nurturing. Strength shaped, not unleashed.
    

The silhouette offers no guidance, only watches.

### **Choice**

Hog must **step toward one path** — or **stand still and face the uncertainty** of shaping his own nature.

### **DM Notes**

- Dawnbinder evaluates _not the path_, but **how Hog views the duality of his growing power**.
    
- Staying still = embracing uncertainty.
    
- Choosing storm = confidence in raw force.
    
- Choosing rain = hope in restraint and growth.

# **🔥 FERGIE — The Flicker Between Worlds (Maria Zurita Echo)**

### **Read-Aloud**

> Gold brightens into blue flame.  
> Heat without burn.  
> A second figure stands beside you, sharing your shadow.

### **Vision**

A silhouette of **blue fire** steps forward, looks similar to Fergies silhouette but slightly off, blurred, echoing in and out of existence like a mismatched reflection.

The silhouette offers Fergie a hand.

Behind her, two scenes shimmer:

1. **A world where Fergie never left home** — safety, smallness, a life lived quietly.
    
2. **A world where Fergie became someone feared** — wild flame, uncontrolled, a name spoken in caution.
    

A voice doubles with static, asking:

> “Who are you choosing to become?”

### **Choice**

Fergie can reach for the hand, step toward one of the flickering worlds, or refuse all three and face the future without anchoring to any vision.

### **DM Notes**

- Dawnbinder evaluates **whether Fergie hopes for connection**, or **chooses independence**, or **accepts not knowing who they may become**.
    
- None of these choices change the “future” — only reveal how Fergie relates to identity and possibility.

# **🌲 OSCAR — The Terrarium & the Broken Veil**

### **Read-Aloud**

> Light bends like glass.  
> You see tiny shapes growing — roots, moss, fragile life suspended in air.

### **Vision**

Oscar’s terrarium floats before him, growing and shrinking erratically as if reacting to the Veil’s instability.

Inside it:

- A tiny warped replica of Oscar stands motionless.
    
- The glass cracks in three places.
    
- Harmonies swirl faintly
    

A whisper of future possibility manifests:

> “What will you nurture… when the world no longer holds shape?”

Three shifts occur:

1. The terrarium grows large enough for Oscar to step inside — **a future of isolation and study**.
    
2. The terrarium collapses into wild roots — **a future of becoming part of the forest**.
    
3. The terrarium mends itself with impossible sigils — **a future tied to the Veil’s mysteries**.
    

### **Choice**

Oscar chooses which version he steps toward, or he can refuse them and remain firmly in the present.

### **DM Notes**

- Dawnbinder evaluates **Oscar’s attitude toward change**:
    
    - Curiosity?
        
    - Fear?
        
    - Duty?
        
    - Rejection of predetermined paths?
        
- No version is “correct.” What matters is how Oscar **faces the unknown harmonics of the Veil**.

# **🩸 ZENNOR — The Blade & the Absence**

### **Read-Aloud**

> Light fades to crimson-gold.  
> A chair sits in the center of the space.  
> And on it — a bracelet you know too well.

### **Vision**

Zennor sees:

- His daughter’s bracelet.
    
- A shadowed outline of his wife — never quite forming into a full figure.
    
- A weapon at his feet, gleaming with unreal clarity.
    

Three visions flicker around him:

1. **Revenge fulfilled** — a shadow falls before him, but the cost is unseen.
    
2. **Justice unresolved** — the path ahead is blank, offering no answers.
    
3. **Forgiveness** — the outline turns away, dissolving into mist.
    

The Dawnbinder’s whisper:

> “What future do you hope for… when grief offers no certain path?”

### **Choice**

Zennor must **touch the bracelet**, **pick up the weapon**, **turn away**, or **stand motionless and accept uncertainty**.

### **DM Notes**

- Dawnbinder reads **Zennor’s relationship to grief**, not morality.
    
- Any choice is valid — the test is in the **emotional truth** Zennor reveals.
