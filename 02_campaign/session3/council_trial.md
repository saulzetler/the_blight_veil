# **1. OVERVIEW**

This trial is designed to:

- Introduce each Council member’s philosophy
    
- Judge the party’s intent, not their power
    
- Reflect the world’s harmonics & Veil instability
    
- Give each PC a spotlight moment
    
- Lead cleanly into the Council’s actual request
    

**The Council is not physically here.**  
They send their presence through **harmonic imprinting** on eight sigils grown into the chamber’s walls.

The chamber responds to the party’s actions:

- Brightening
    
- Darkening
    
- Shifting temperature
    
- Changing tone
    
- Resonating with harmonics (Oscar especially)
    

The trial can be completed in ~30–45 minutes at the table.

# **2. CHAMBER INTRODUCTION**

## **Read-Aloud**

> The ascent ends in a hollow chamber deep within the heartwood. Eight sigils spiral across the curved walls, each etched so deep the bark seems grown around them.
> 
> No Council members wait inside.  
> Only presence.
> 
> A hum stirs in the wood — low, ancient, unsettling. The sigils brighten one by one, like eyes opening in a dream.
> 
> The trial begins.

---

# **3. CHAMBER MECHANICS**

### **Lighting & Sound**

- Light is soft, shifting like lantern-glow through leaves
    
- Harmonic tones hum subtly in the background
    
- Each trial shifts both light and sound
    

### **How the Chamber Scores**

Track successes/failures privately:

- **Success** = +1
    
- **Partial** = 0
    
- **Failure** = –1
    

At the end of all eight:

- **+3 or higher** → Harmonious
    
- **+1 to +2** → Mixed
    
- **0 or below** → Disharmonious
    

All paths still progress the story.

# **5. DETAILED MANIFESTATIONS**

---

## **🌱 1. ROOTKEEPER — Truth of Intent**

### **Chamber Shift**

Green sigil glows. Roots rise gently and coil around wrists.

### **Read-Aloud**

> The roots pulse softly. A vibration — not a voice — murmurs through the bark:  
> “Why have you come?”

### **Test**

Each PC gives their reason for seeking the Council.

**Checks:**

- Persuasion/Insight (DC 12) → Success
    
- Deception (DC 15) → Success
    
- Failure → the root constricts slightly; lights dim
    

### **Consequences**

- Success: buds bloom on the walls
    
- Failure: bark darkens slightly
    

---

## **🔥 2. EMBER SPEAKER — Courage**

### **Chamber Shift**

Heat rises. Sap glows like coals.

### **Read-Aloud**

> Fire blooms from nowhere — a sweeping wave, bright but not burning.

### **Test**

Stand firm or act boldly.

**Checks:**

- Wisdom Save (DC 13) → Success
    
- Or a bold action → Success
    

### **Consequences**

- Success: flame curves away
    
- Failure: ember-marks appear under their feet
    

Fergie’s emberstone faintly flares.

---

## **🪨 3. STONE TELLER — Harmonic Memory**

### **Chamber Shift**

Crystalline growths sprout, humming with tones.

### **Read-Aloud**

> The chamber vibrates — a call waiting for an answer.

### **Test**

Recreate the monolith’s harmonic.

**Checks:**  
Arcana / Performance / Intelligence (DC 13)

### **Consequences**

- Success: crystals glow cleanly
    
- Failure: crystal snaps → 1 psychic dmg
    

Oscar’s terrarium pulses in sync.

---

## **🕶 4. WHISPERING MASK — Guile & Perception**

### **Chamber Shift**

Light dims; shadows lengthen.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A whisper moves behind you. Or in front. Or above.  
> “Seek what hides.”

### **Test**

Locate an illusion or hidden sigil.

**Checks:**

- Perception (DC 14)
    
- Investigation (DC 13)
    
- Insight (DC 12)
    

### **Consequences**

- Success: shadow recoils, impressed
    
- Failure: a soft laugh echoes — not cruel, just amused
    

---

## **🌊 5. RIVER-SEER — Mercy & Judgment**

### **Chamber Shift**

Illusion-water ripples across the floor.  
A spectral wounded beast appears.

### **Read-Aloud**

> The creature whimpers — half-real, half-memory.  
> The water ripples with a single question:  
> “What will you do?”

### **Test**

Players choose action: help, kill, ignore, question.

No roll.  
The Council reads the **reasoning**.

### **Consequences**

All choices can succeed if justified.  
Cruelty without reason = failure.

Oscar often gets emotional tremors from the terrarium here.

---

## **⚔️ 6. OATH-SEVERED — Discipline of Arms**

### **Chamber Shift**

Walls harden. A vine-construct rises.

### **Read-Aloud**

> Roots braid into a warrior-shape.  
> “Strike with clarity. Defend with purpose.”

### **Test**

One turn each in symbolic combat.

### **Consequences**

Graded on:

- Control
    
- Judgment
    
- Non-lethal creativity
    
- Recklessness penalized
    

Zennor’s amulet hums with cold energy.

---

## **☀️ 7. DAWNBINDER — Hope in Uncertainty**

[[dawnbinder]]

### **Chamber Shift**

Golden-white light floods the space.

### **Read-Aloud**

> Warm light brushes your skin.  
> Shadows fall away.  
> For a moment — a glimpse of a possible future.

### **Test**

PC steps forward or away.

Both accepted.

### **Evaluation**

Judges _how_ they face the unknown.

---

## **💠 8. VEILWATCHER — Understanding the Wound**

### **Chamber Shift**

A sigil of impossible geometry appears.  
Light refracts strangely, like water bending around heat.

### **Read-Aloud**

> The sigil waits — listening.  
> “Speak what you believe the Blight is.”

### **Test**

- Arcana (DC 15)
    
- Nature (DC 13)
    
- Religion (DC 14)
    
- Or pure honesty (auto)
    

### **Consequences**

- Truth: sigil glows
    
- Uncertainty: dim shimmer
    
- Lie: sigil vibrates sharply
    

Oscar’s terrarium answers with a matching flicker.

# **6. GROUP CONSEQUENCES & SCORING**

### **+3 or higher — Harmonious Verdict**

- Passage opens
    
- Council fully accepts meeting
    
- They offer guidance willingly
    

### **+1 to +2 — Mixed Verdict**

- Passage opens slowly
    
- Council will meet them, but some members mildly oppose
    
- Additional scrutiny later
    

### **0 or below — Disharmonious**

- Passage does not open
    
- A root rises offering a mission:
    
    - cleanse a corrupted grove
        
    - help Rootspire stabilize a Veil tremor
        
    - recover a stolen sigil tablet
        

After completing it, they may return.

This is not failure — just pacing.

# **8. POST-TRIAL — COUNCIL VERDICT**

## **Read-Aloud (Harmonious or Mixed)**

> The sigils brighten in unison.  
> The chamber exhales — warm, relieved.  
> Roots spiral upward, revealing a narrow ascent lit by soft green light.
> 
> The Council will see you now.

## **Read-Aloud (Disharmonious)**

> The sigils dim.  
> The chamber grows still — not rejecting, simply waiting.  
> A single root rises from the floor, offering a token carved from pale wood.
> 
> “Prove yourselves. Return when the forest speaks your name.”
