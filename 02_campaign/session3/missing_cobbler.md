# **Side Quest: “The Missing Cobbler of Rootspire”**

## **Premise**

Rootspire’s beloved sandal-maker, **Bramble Tark**, has disappeared. His stall sits abandoned on one of the upper root paths, his tools scattered among moss and sap-leather. Locals whisper that he wandered off “following a strange hum beneath the bark.” Recent instability in the Veil has made Rootspire’s under-routes shift—making the search both dangerous and strange.

---

# **Read-Aloud**

> A shuttered stall clings to the spiraling root-path, its moss awning drooping. Wooden lasts, scrap-leather, and half-studded sandals lie scattered as if dropped mid-motion. A faint hum pulses beneath the counter—irregular, uneasy, like a heartbeat echoing through wood. The air smells of pine resin and damp soil. An unfinished flip-flop sits alone on the workbench, its sigil glowing a faint, unsteady violet.

---

# **DM Notes**

- Bramble is harmless, eccentric, and deeply attuned to Rootspire’s living pathways.
    
- He believes shoes “remember” the paths they walk, and he listens to them for inspiration.
    
- A **blight pocket beneath Rootspire** resonated with the sigils on one of his prototypes, pulling him downward.
    
- This quest adds humor, light tension, and ties into Veil instability.
    

---

# **Clues & Leads**

## **At Bramble’s Stall**

### **Skill Checks**

- **Investigation DC 13** – Scrape marks and dropped tools show he left _voluntarily_, but in sudden fascination.
    
- **Arcana DC 14** – The unfinished flip-flop’s sigil is trying to “complete” itself, pointing _downwards_ through the root.
    
- **Nature DC 12** – The root beneath the stall is stressed, its growth pattern warped by faint blight.
    
- **Survival DC 12** – A set of small footprints leads toward a maintenance nook between two thick root-pillars.
    

### **NPC Conversations**

- **Lanthi (mushroom-seller):** _“He kept talkin’ about a new sound down below. Said it made the bark… shimmer.”_
    
- **Two bored teens with reed flutes:** _“We saw him slip through a hollow. Looked real focused. Creepy, honestly.”_
    
- **Old barkshaper:** _“Sometimes the roots shift. Sometimes they call. Ain’t wise to follow.”_
    

---

# **Exploration: The Under-Roots**

A narrow passage spiraling down into the living structure beneath Rootspire. The hum intensifies, unstable, tinged with violet harmonics that flicker like veins of light.

## **Read-Aloud**

> The tunnel tightens as you descend, bark bending in slow waves as though breathing. Thin seams of violet shimmer pulse through the wood. The air warms, then chills in rhythmic pulses. Every step carries the echo of Bramble’s path—hurried, curious, entranced.

---

# **Encounter 1: Harmonic Drift (Puzzle)**

### **The Issue**

The under-routes have warped. A loop of corridor folds onto itself unless the party aligns with the unstable harmonic of Bramble’s prototype sandal.

### **Puzzle**

Players either pick up the sandal from the stand or find one down the hatch.

**Skill Checks:**

- ** Arcana or Perception DC 13** – Feel the sandal vibrating
    
- **Nature DC 12** – “Feel” the living root’s rhythm and steady it.
    

**Hints:**

The sandal vibrates slightly when pointed the correct direction.

**Failure:**  
They loop back and lose 1 hour. One random PC gains **temporary fatigue** (disadvantage on first check in next encounter).

---

# **Encounter 2: Blightling Swarm**

### **Creatures**

**Blight twigs**

- **Skitter Pulse (Reaction):** When struck, a nearby blightling shifts 10 ft.
    
- **Harmonic Rattle (Recharge 5–6):** 10 ft tremor; **DC 13 Dex save** or fall prone.
    

### **Avoidance**

A **Stealth DC 14** can bypass them if players muffle their sounds with moss or cloth.

---

# **Finding Bramble**

## **The Blighted Hollow**

A small cavern where the root has warped inward, pulsing with violet veins—the blight pocket trying to “sing” through the tree.

Bramble kneels before it, humming back at an off-key resonance.

### **Read-Aloud**

> A hunched figure sits before a swollen knot of twisted bark. Violet veins pulse beneath the surface like bruised light. Bramble breathes in time with it, humming softly—wrongly—his voice fraying with each note. His mismatched sandals glow faintly, absorbing and returning tiny fragments of the blight’s rhythm.

---

# **Resolution Options**

### **Skill-Based**

- **Persuasion DC 14** – Call him back to himself.
    
- **Arcana DC 12** – Break the resonance safely.
    
- **Medicine DC 13** – Help him breathe properly and disrupt the trance.
    
- **Strength DC 15** – Pull him away, though this risks backlash.
    

### **If Backlash Occurs**

The blight-pocket lashes out.  
Use **1–2 Needle Blights** with +1 attack and +1d4 psychic damage.

### **Outcomes**

**Success:**  
Bramble collapses into their arms, exhausted but unharmed.  
He insists on gifting them a pair of his “experimental flip-flops.”

**Partial Success:**  
The blight stings him; he suffers mild confusion for an hour but is recoverable.

**Failure:**  
The blight swells and explodes DC 15 dexterity save 1d10 force damage half on save.

# **Reward: Bramble’s Experimental Flip-Flops**

## **Flip-Flops of Fortunate Missteps**

_Wondrous Item (Uncommon, no attunement)_

**Properties:**

- **Plop-Step:** Once per long rest, teleport **10 ft** with a faint comedic _plop_.
    
- **Surefooted:** Advantage on ability checks to balance or traverse natural terrain (roots, vines, moss).
    
- **Squeaky Curse:** On stone/metal floors, they squeak unless the wearer passes a **DC 10 Stealth** check each round.
    
- **One Perfect Step:** Once per long rest, add +5 to a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check.
    
- **Slippery Freedom:** Automatically escape nonmagical restraining vines or roots—the flip-flops slide off with a ridiculous _fwip_ then reappear on the wearer’s feet.
