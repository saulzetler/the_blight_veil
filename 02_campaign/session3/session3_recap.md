## Session 3 

- After defeating the forest threat, the party searched the remains and recovered:
    
    - A **strange empty vial**
        
    - A **lantern that can be powered**, though its method of activation is unknown
        
- During a long rest, **Zennor experienced a vivid and unsettling dream**
    
- Rebel allies instructed the party to travel **east toward the great tree city**
    
- Overnight, the rebels’ **makeshift river bridge was swept away**
    
- To cross the river:
    
    - **Hog swam across**, securing a rope on the far bank
        
    - **Oscar identified a massive tree** that could serve as a bridge if felled
        
    - Zennor’s first attempt to cut it down failed when his sword bounced off the trunk
        
    - The rebels agreed to **lend the party an axe**
        
    - Zennor struck again and **felled the tree cleanly**, creating a bridge
        
- While crossing:
    
    - Zennor slipped near the far end and was left **hanging from the rope**
        
    - Hog was distracted and unable to help
        
    - **Fergie rushed forward and pulled Zennor to safety**
        
    - Oscar followed, and the party crossed successfully
        
- The party **said farewell to the rebels** and continued east
    
- In a forest clearing, they encountered an **unresponsive, blight-covered old man**
    
    - Combat broke out as the man advanced on them
        
    - During the fight, he begged Oscar for help before lashing out again
        
    - Oscar’s **terrarium began glowing green**, easing the blight
        
    - When held close, the blight **fully evaporated**
        
    - The man thanked Oscar with his last breath
        
    - Oscar realized the terrarium can **purify blight**
        
- The party arrived at the great tree city of **Rootspire**
    
    - Masked sentries questioned their intentions
        
    - After explaining their journey and desire to see the **Council of Eight**, they were allowed entry
        
- Inside Rootspire:
    
    - The party chose the tavern over the market
        
    - **Hog and Zennor competed in ale chugging contests**, ending in a tie
        
    - They played dice with **Briar and Fern**, also ending evenly
        
- At the market:
    
    - Zennor performed and received an item that **resonates with a steady humming frequency**
        
    - Oscar purchased a **moss patch that purifies nearby air**
        
    - The party discovered an **abandoned cobbler’s stall**
        
    - Found a **magical but unfinished flip-flop**
        
    - Uncovered a **hidden trapdoor** leading into the roots below
        
- Beneath Rootspire:
    
    - The party slipped past blighted twigs
        
    - Found **Bramble Tark** transfixed by a patch of blight
        
    - Bramble was humming **out of tune with the blight’s harmonics**
        
    - Zennor used his **harmonic tuning reed** to match the blight’s frequency
        
    - Bramble was freed from the blight’s influence
        
    - He rewarded the party with **experimental flip-flops**
        
- The party ascended to the **council chamber**
    
    - After persuading wardens, they were granted entry
        
- The **Council of Eight** tested the party:
    
    - **Rootkeeper** questioned their true intent
        
    - **Ember Speaker** tested courage with illusory fire
        
    - **Stone Teller** summoned harmonic crystal memory
        
    - **Whispering Mask** tested perception through illusion
        
    - **River Seer** asked for mercy or judgment over a wounded creature
        
    - **Oath-Severed** set a non-hostile combat trial
        
    - **Dawnbinder** showed each party member visions of possible futures:
        
        - Hog saw paths of violent storm and gentle rain, choosing neither
            
        - Fergie saw a life of quiet safety and one of feared flame, choosing neither
            
        - Oscar saw three terrarium futures and ultimately chose isolation and study
            
        - Zennor saw his family and a gleaming weapon, which he seized and used
            
    - **Veil Watcher** asked each member what they believe the Blight truly is
        
- The council voted:
    
    - All approved **except the Whispering Mask and the Oath-Severed**
        
- With a majority in favor, the party was granted an **official audience with the Council**
