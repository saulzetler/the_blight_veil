# Campaign Overview: The Blight Veil

This document provides a high-level summary of the campaign's progress, key events, and current status.

## Narrative Arc Summary

The campaign follows a group of unlikely allies who fled the Concord capital of **Orrenval** after killing a high-ranking captain. They have traveled east into the **Ashroot**, a rebel-held forest region, where they are now investigating supernatural anomalies known as the **Veil** under the mandate of the mysterious **Council of Eight** in the tree-city of **Rootspire**.

---

## Session History

### Session 1: The Spark
- **Setting:** Orrenval, the Concord Capital.
- **Key Events:** 
    - The party interrupted a heresy trial, killing **Captain Deyran**.
    - Met **Vaereth** of the **Cindersong** rebels.
    - Witnessed a **Veil Event** (a tear in reality) while escaping the city.
    - Oscar (05C4R) discovered his serial number and basic identity.

### Session 2: Into the Ashroot
- **Setting:** The wilds between Orrenval and the Ashroot.
- **Key Events:** 
    - Found a hidden Cindersong vault containing relics (**Emberheart Pendant**, **Cindersong Blade**, **Song of the Veiled Choir**).
    - Established contact with rebel scouts (**Rilwen**, **Cadran**, **Iraen**).
    - Defeated a Concord patrol whose leader mutated into a blighted brute using a strange ichor.

### Session 3: The Great Tree
- **Setting:** The River crossing and Rootspire.
- **Key Events:** 
    - Oscar used his **terrarium** to purify a blighted man, discovering its unique power.
    - Arrived at **Rootspire** and underwent a psychological trial by the **Council of Eight**.
    - Each member received a vision of their potential future/arc.
    - Freed **Bramble Tark** from blight in the lower roots.

### Session 4: The Council's Mandate
- **Setting:** Rootspire Council Chamber.
- **Key Events:** 
    - The Council tasked the party with observing Veil anomalies.
    - Encountered the **Whispering Mask**, a council member who demanded "PURIFICATION" against the others' wishes.
    - Received the **Anomaly Reliquary (Jaihroquai)**, which points toward Veil sources.
    - Learned that Orrenval is in open revolt following their actions in Session 1.
    - Recruited **Timoor** (aka Tumor).

### Session 5: The Lower Voice
- **Setting:** The Lower Roots of Rootspire and the Listening Rock.
- **Key Events:** 
    - Descended into the "Hollow Nave" and encountered **Vessel-Null**, an ancient, blighted construct of the same model as Oscar.
    - Learned about **"The Drift"** and **"Project Dawnswell"** (a secret Concord initiative).
    - Observed the **Listening Rock**, where Oscar's terrarium synced with the pulse of the Veil.
    - Discovered fresh Concord military tracks; the enemy is already deep in the Ashroot, closer than reported.

## Current Status & Threads
- **Location:** Returning to Rootspire from the Listening Rock.
- **Immediate Threat:** A Concord Harmonic Assessment unit is active in the area.
- **Mystery:** What is "The Drift" that Vessel-Null warned Oscar about?
- **Political:** Orrenval is burning; the Trifarix is fractured.
- **Personal:** 
    - **Zennor:** Might have seen a vision of his wife, Maren.
    - **Hog:** Invited to the "Vindication Rounds" (Concord arenas).
    - **Fergie:** Tracking a "woman in smoke" (Maria Zurita).
    - **Oscar:** Discovering his origins as a "Vessel" for containment.
